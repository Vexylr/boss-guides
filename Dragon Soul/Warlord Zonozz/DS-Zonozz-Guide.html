Overview

Warlord Zon'ozz is a void lord and the second boss of Dragon Soul. He summons a Void of the Unmaking (void orb) that travels toward the raid. Players must bounce the orb back and forth between assigned groups â€” if it hits the wall, the raid is launched into the air (wipe). Each time the orb collides with players, Void Diffusion triggers: ~180,000 Shadow damage split among nearby players, the orb ricochets, and its damage increases +20% per bounce. Stack in groups to share the damage. When ready, the raid lets the orb hit Zon'ozz to trigger Phase 2: everything goes dark and adds spawn; after that, return to Phase 1. On Heroic, Disrupting Shadows adds a dispel or cooldown requirement.

Phases & Mechanics

Void of the Unmaking (Orb) [N/H]

  • Zon'ozz summons the Void of the Unmaking â€” an orb that travels toward the raid.
  • When the orb hits players, Void Diffusion occurs: ~180,000 Shadow damage split evenly among nearby players, the orb ricochets in the opposite direction, and the orb's damage increases by 20% per diffusion (it absorbs souls).
  • Stack in assigned groups (e.g. melee stack, ranged stack) so each bounce is shared. Bounce the orb back and forth between groups. Do not let it hit the wall â€” that launches the raid into the air (wipe).
  • The raid leader decides when to let the orb hit Zon'ozz. When it touches the boss, Phase 2 begins (dark room, adds). After Phase 2 ends, Phase 1 resumes with a new orb. Typically you bounce several times to build damage on the boss when the orb eventually hits him, then handle Phase 2.
⚠
Positioning: Two groups (e.g. melee and ranged), each stacked so Void Diffusion is split among 5â€“6 (10-man) or 12â€“13 (25-man). Alternate which group intercepts the orb so it bounces between them. Call out "orb to melee" / "orb to ranged" so the correct group is stacked when the orb arrives.

  • Phase 2 is triggered only when the orb touches Zon'ozz (not at a fixed HP%). The room goes dark; adds spawn.
  • Kill the adds and survive the phase. When it ends, you return to Phase 1 and a new orb spawns. Repeat until the boss is dead.
