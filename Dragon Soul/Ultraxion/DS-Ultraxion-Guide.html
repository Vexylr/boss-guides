Overview

Ultraxion is the fifth boss of Dragon Soul. Roughly every 45 seconds he casts Hour of Twilight — a raid-wide hit (~300k) that kills anyone who doesn't avoid it. Everyone receives Heroic Will (extra action button): use it before Hour of Twilight to phase into safety (you cannot move or attack for ~5 seconds). Tanking is usually solo — Fading Light does not stack like a traditional swap debuff; instead it forces precise Heroic Will usage. Clicking Heroic Will drops your threat and briefly prevents regaining it, so tanks must wait for another player to pull aggro before taunting again. Thrall grants tanks Last Defender of Azeroth. Single-phase burn; 6-minute enrage. Server note: some private realms (e.g. Whitemane) intentionally tune Heroic Ultraxion to ~124M HP (10-player) instead of the classic ~78M — verify your combat log.

⚠ Enrage
  • 6-minute enrage. Ultraxion gains Fury. This is a strict DPS check; the raid must kill him before 6 minutes.
  • Hour of Twilight ~every 45s — use Heroic Will to avoid. Fading Light targets tanks (and sometimes DPS/healers on Heroic) — it is not a stacking debuff; swaps are driven by Fading Light + Heroic Will timing, not “two stacks.” Thrall gives tanks Last Defender of Azeroth.
  • Threat: Using Heroic Will clears threat briefly. The next tank (or a designated soaker) must let their taunt land only after someone has picked the boss, or Ultraxion will melee random players.

Phase 1 — Full Fight Mechanics

Hour of Twilight & Heroic Will [N/H]

  • Roughly every 45 seconds Ultraxion casts Hour of Twilight — a raid-wide hit (~300k damage). Anyone not avoiding it dies.
  • Everyone receives Heroic Will (extra action button). Use it before Hour of Twilight lands to phase into safety; you cannot move or attack for ~5 seconds, then you return. Heroic Will has a cooldown (~5s) — use it only when Hour of Twilight is imminent.
  • Call out "Hour of Twilight" so the raid uses Heroic Will in time. Don't wait until the last second; use it with 2–3s to spare to avoid lag or movement issues.
⚠
Timing: Use Heroic Will when Hour of Twilight is about to cast (or when the debuff/cast bar shows it). While phased you cannot DPS or heal — that's acceptable to avoid death.

Fading Light, threat & tanking [N/H]

  • Fading Light marks players for deletion unless they use Heroic Will inside the debuff window. It does not stack for swap purposes — think of it as a parallel timer to Hour of Twilight, not a Sundering-style stack.
  • Solo tanking is the default strat: one tank holds Ultraxion the entire fight while healers pump massive heals. If you run two tanks, swaps happen when Fading Light/Hour cadences require it — never taunt immediately after your co-tank presses Heroic Will or you will rip aggro during their immunity window and die to melee.
  • Pressing Heroic Will causes a short threat suppression. Wait until another player (backup tank, pet, or misdirect target) has boss aggro before taunting back.
  • Thrall grants tanks Last Defender of Azeroth — use it to help survive tank damage and Hour of Twilight timing.

Raid damage & burn [N/H]

  • Ultraxion deals moderate raid-wide damage over time. Healers keep the raid topped. There are no adds and no phase changes — pure single-target DPS on Ultraxion.
  • Use Lust/Heroism at the pull or after the first Fading Light cycle when everyone is present. All DPS and healers (when not in the portal) focus the boss until he dies.
♔

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

Heroic increases damage and often applies Fading Light to DPS and healers as well as tanks — everyone must track the debuff. HP values may be custom (see health table note). Threat suppression after Heroic Will is more punishing; assign a backup pick-up (Hunter pet, Army ghoul, etc.) if you solo tank.

⚠
Heroic tip: WeakAuras for Hour + Fading on every player. Call “taunt in 2” only after the previous Heroic Will user is phased. Save personal DPS cooldowns for windows where you are not locked in a portal.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Solo or duo — no stack-based swap; follow Fading/HoT cadence
  • After Heroic Will, pause before taunting; let aggro stabilize
  • Last Defender on CD for scary overlaps
⚔ DPS
  • Full burn on Ultraxion at all times
  • When you get Fading Light, use Heroic Will before it expires
  • Return from portal and resume DPS immediately
💉 Healers
  • Heal raid and tank through constant damage
  • When you get Fading Light, use Heroic Will before it expires
  • Top raid before Fading Light expires in case someone is slow
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use at the pull for maximum uptime. Everyone is present and no one is in the portal yet. This maximizes the burn before enrage.
