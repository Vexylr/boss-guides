Overview

Warmaster Blackhorn is the sixth boss of Dragon Soul on the Skyfire. [N] Phase 1: drakes and Goriona in the air — kill drakes, soak or split Twilight Barrage, keep the ship alive. Phase 2: Blackhorn lands on the deck; finish him while handling his mechanics. [H] Goriona lands on the deck (she does not land on Normal in the same way). While she is airborne she applies heavy deck fire and AoE pressure; the ship often takes no player-targetable “hull damage” from the old Normal pattern — watch for Deck Sappers when transitions occur. On Heroic, deck fire cannot be extinguished by players; NPC firefighters put it out (especially after Blackhorn lands). Running through fire spreads it. Blackhorn landing does not spawn a wave of melee adds — the danger is Goriona’s fire from above until she lands. At ~90% HP Goriona lands on the deck; at ~40% she flees. Do not bring Blackhorn’s DPS so high that he kills Goriona before she flees — if he does, he heals from it. Heroic also uses a healing absorb debuff on a random player (see phases).

⚠
Twilight Barrage & ship (Normal) [N] — Drakes and Goriona pressure the ship with barrages. Kill drakes in time and soak/split damage so the hull survives to Phase 2.

Heroic Goriona + fire [H] — Respect 90% land / 40% flee timing; avoid killing Goriona early. Deck fire is lethal if mishandled — do not spread it across the deck. Heal the Consuming Shroud-style absorb debuff slowly with AoE heals while the player stays in the raid (see Phase 2).

Phases & Mechanics

Phase 1 — Ship / air defense [N/H]

  • [N] Twilight drakes fly in — kill them before Twilight Barrage nukes the hull. Goriona assists from range; assign ranged DPS and harpoon/gunners per your strat.
  • [H] Deck fire appears on Heroic only — it cannot be clicked off by players. Firefighter NPCs remove patches (especially once Blackhorn is on deck). Do not run through fire — you spread fresh patches.
  • [H] While Goriona is still flying, she peppers the deck with AoE — there is no separate “Warmaster landing add wave”; the air phase is about drakes, barrages, fire, and positioning. A Deck Sapper may appear around landing — kill it quickly.
  • Phase 1 ends when Blackhorn leaps to the deck (trigger timing varies slightly by mode — watch the emote/cinematic).
⚠
Barrage (Normal): Drake kill order and soak groups save the ship. (Heroic): Treat fire like a second boss — kite Blackhorn/Goriona away from fresh patches and let firefighters work.

Phase 2 — Blackhorn on deck [N/H]

  • Warmaster Blackhorn tanks on the deck; dodge Twilight Onslaught (huge purple swirls — move out). [N] Goriona may assist from range depending on version. [H] Goriona lands at ~90% HP, participates in the deck fight, then flees at ~40% — stop DPS on her as she approaches flee range so Blackhorn doesn’t execute her and heal to full.
  • [H] Consuming Shroud / Siphon Soul-style debuff: a random player gets a healing absorb (~100k on some private servers). Any healing that removes absorb also mirrors damage to the entire raid — heal it slowly with steady AoE (player stays stacked). If the player dies with absorb remaining, the rest hits everyone at once (example: 20k healed of 100k then death → ~80k raid splash). Sending the player aside to funnel heals can work, but stack + AoE heals is the simplest default.
  • Finish Blackhorn after Goriona leaves; avoid fire and Onslaught until the encounter ends.
♔

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

Heroic is a different encounter rhythm: Goriona on deck, persistent fire, healing absorb on the raid, and a hard requirement to let Goriona flee instead of dying to cleave. Drake damage and Barrage tuning are higher — check your server’s exact values.

⚠
Heroic tip: Call Goriona HP for stop-DPS. Rotate raid CDs for slow absorb healing windows. Save lust for a stable deck moment after Goriona lands or after fire is under control — not during chaotic air RNG unless you must.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Phase 1: Tank adds that land on deck; protect ship
  • Phase 2: Tank Blackhorn (and Goriona if needed); face Blackhorn away
  • Mitigate Twilight Onslaught; swap if debuff stacks
⚔ DPS
  • Phase 1: Kill drakes before Twilight Barrage [N]; handle fire lines [H]
  • Phase 2: Blackhorn + Goriona — [H] stop on Goriona at flee HP
  • Use ship guns/harpoon as provided
💉 Healers
  • Phase 1: Barrage / fire / drake damage
  • Phase 2: Onslaught + [H] slow AoE on absorb debuff
  • Do not panic-heal the debuff — smooth throughput
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use at the start of Phase 2 when Blackhorn and Goriona land, and the raid is grouped. Or use in Phase 1 if drake waves are overwhelming — ensure Barrage doesn't land.
