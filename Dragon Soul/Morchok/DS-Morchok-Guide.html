Overview

Morchok is a massive stone giant and the first boss of Dragon Soul. The fight alternates between a normal phase and a Black Blood phase. In normal: Stomp deals heavy Physical damage â€” the raid must stack near the boss (within ~25 yards) so damage is spread; the two closest players (tanks) take double Stomp damage. Resonating Crystal spawns periodically: three players run to the crystal and stand there until it explodes (90k Shadow split among them); the beam turns blue when close enough. Tank swap Crush Armor. After two crystals explode, Morchok enters Black Blood: he pulls the raid, stuns, drops earth spikes (Earth's Vengeance); run behind a spike and avoid the black blood, then wait for his Stomp before running back in. At 20% he enrages. [H] At 90% he splits into two bosses (Morchok + Kohcrom); split the raid and handle each separately.

Phase 1 â€” Full Fight Mechanics

Stomp [N/H]

  • Stomp deals heavy Physical damage. Damage is split among all players within 25 yards of Morchok.
  • Everyone must stack close to the boss (within 25 yards) so the damage is shared. The two closest players (the tanks) take double the per-player share â€” that is intentional; keep tanks closest.
  • If the raid is spread, Stomp can one-shot. Always regroup on the boss for each Stomp.

Resonating Crystal [N/H]

  • Morchok summons a Resonating Crystal roughly 35â€“40 yards away. After ~12 seconds it explodes, dealing ~90,000 Shadow damage split among the three closest players.
  • Assign three players to run to the crystal and stand there until it explodes. The beam linking player to crystal turns blue when the player is close enough.
  • Keep the crystal close enough to the boss so the rest of the raid can still be in 25 yards of Morchok for Stomp (tank may need to position boss near the crystal so melee are in range for both).

Crush Armor [N/H]

  • Morchok applies Crush Armor to the current tank: stacking debuff âˆ’10% armor per stack (up to 10 stacks, ~20s duration).
  • Tank swap when the debuff fades off the other tank, or around 4 stacks. Off-tank taunts; main tank steps out to drop stacks.

Black Blood Phase (after 2 crystals) [N/H]

  • After the second Resonating Crystal explodes, Morchok uses The Earth Consumes You!: pulls all players to him, 5% max HP as Physical damage per second for 5 seconds, and summons Earth's Vengeance â€” shards erupt from the ground (15k Physical to anyone within 2 yards) and leave behind obstacles (spikes).
  • When the stun wears off, run to a spike and hide behind it. Black Blood of the Earth erupts outward â€” 5,000 Nature damage per second and +100% Nature damage taken per second while standing in the blood (stacks to 20). Stay out of the blood.
  • When he finishes channeling the blood, he will immediately Stomp. Do not run back to the boss until after that Stomp or you will die. Then hug the boss again and repeat the normal rotation.

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

🔭 Kohcrom â€” Two Bosses
  • At 90% health, Morchok splits into two bosses: Morchok and Kohcrom. Kohcrom casts the same abilities as Morchok but a few seconds later.
  • Split the raid â€” typically two 5-man teams (10-man) or two ~12â€“13 (25-man), one per boss. Each side needs a tank, heals, and DPS. Separate the bosses (clear trash on the sides of the lane if needed for space).
  • First Resonating Crystal belongs to the Morchok team. The Kohcrom tank drags Kohcrom out of range of that crystal while the Morchok team soaks it.
  • On each side, one non-tank should stack on the tank to take the double Stomp damage (so it's predictable and one person soaks it with the tank). The other three players stack on the Resonating Crystal when it's their turn. Assign a player with good survivability for the double-Stomp soak.
  • The tank should run toward the Resonating Crystal when it spawns so melee are in range of both the boss and the crystal.
  • Use the Black Blood phase to retreat to the center and reset boss positions so the fight doesn't creep into adds.
⚠
Heroic tip: Crush Armor still applies per boss â€” each tank swaps with their off-tank or when debuff fades. Some groups use 3 or 4 healers for safety.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Be the two closest to boss for Stomp (you take double share)
  • Swap Crush Armor when debuff fades or ~4 stacks
  • [H] One tank per boss; drag your boss to crystal when it's your team's turn
⚔ DPS / Soaks
  • Stack on boss (within 25y) for every Stomp
  • Assigned 3 run to each Resonating Crystal; beam turns blue when close
  • Black Blood: hide behind spike, avoid blood, don't run in until after his Stomp
💉 Healers
  • Heal through shared Stomp and Crystal soak damage
  • Crush Armor tank damage; save CDs for 20% enrage
  • [H] Split heals between the two boss groups
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Save for the 20% enrage phase when damage spikes, or use early if your DPS is comfortable. Black Blood phase is a good time to regroup.
