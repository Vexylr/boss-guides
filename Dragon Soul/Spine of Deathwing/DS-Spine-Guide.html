Overview

Spine of Deathwing is the seventh encounter in Dragon Soul. The raid fights on his back across three armor plates. On each plate you must peel the plate with a Nuclear Blast from a Hideous Amalgamation that has soaked Residue from dead Corrupted Blood (typically 9 stacks of Absorbed Blood), then burn the exposed Burning Tendons before they seal. There is no player “grip” button — staying alive during Barrel Roll is about where you stand and, when your strat uses it, the Fiery Grip channel from a living Corruption (DoT + tether to the spine). After three tendon burns (one per plate), the fight ends.

⚠
Barrel Roll [N/H]
Deathwing performs Barrel Roll when his back is weighted unevenly (players split left vs right). Anyone on the wrong side when he rolls is thrown off and dies. Typical approach: stack the entire raid in one armor-hole / corner so weight stays predictable, and control when you allow a roll (e.g. to clear Residue or reset tentacles) with a clear call. During a roll, players grabbed by Fiery Grip stay tethered to the spine (heavy raid damage — heal through it). There is no special action bar “Grip” ability given to players.
Plate 1
First armor plate
Manage Corruptions; kill Corrupted Blood to spawn Residue; build a Hideous Amalgamation to 9 Absorbed Blood; kill it on the plate for Nuclear Blast; burn Burning Tendons. Control Barrel Roll with stacks / Fiery Grip as planned.
Plate 2
Second armor plate
Same loop: amalgamation soak → Nuclear Blast on plate → tendon DPS window. Rolls and Corruption spawns reset the puzzle; repeat positioning discipline.
Plate 3
Final armor plate
Third tendon burn finishes the encounter. Heroic adds Blood Corruption: Death / Earth dispel routing across all three plates.

⚠ Wipe conditions
  • No fixed enrage; you lose to Barrel Roll throws, Nuclear Blast mistakes, failed tendon DPS inside the burn window, or (Heroic) Blood Corruption: Death explosions.
  • Each of the three plates follows the same mechanical loop: control Corruptions and rolls → soak Residue to 9 Absorbed Blood on an amalgamation → Nuclear Blast the plate → kill Burning Tendons before they submerge.

Phases & Mechanics (per plate)

Core units [N/H]

  • Corruption — Large tentacle. Searing Plasma puts a healing absorb and periodic Physical damage on random players (assign healers to brute-force heals until it clears). Fiery Grip is a long channel on a player (stun + heavy Fire ticks); break it by dealing a slice of the Corruption's health (about 15–20% in one burst — avoid slowly grinding the add down unless you intend to kill it). Killing a Corruption spawns a Hideous Amalgamation and enables Corrupted Blood spawns from that hole.
  • Corrupted Blood — Small add. On death leaves a pool of Residue on the spine. A Hideous Amalgamation walking through Residue gains Absorbed Blood (stacks, more damage taken per stack).
  • Hideous Amalgamation — Tanked add. At 9 stacks of Absorbed Blood, killing it next to an armor plate triggers Nuclear Blast, tearing the plate open so Burning Tendons can be damaged. If it dies with fewer stacks, the blast is too weak to expose the tendon reliably — reset and keep soaking.
  • Burning Tendons — Only vulnerable target while the plate is peeled. Short window; all DPS and cooldowns go here. You need three successful tendon burns (one per plate) to clear the encounter.

Barrel Roll & Fiery Grip [N/H]

  • Barrel Roll happens when Deathwing senses uneven weight on his left vs right halves. Keep the raid stacked in one hole / agreed corner for predictable control; splitting the raid across both halves causes a roll after a brief delay.
  • Common setup: kill three Corruptions so one hole is free, leave one Corruption alive (often the one opposite your stack). The raid stacks in a predetermined corner / hole. For a Barrel Roll, players can also stand in open armor holes to gain Grasping Tendrils (a short tether that glues you to the spine). When a Fiery Grip channel is intentionally left up through a roll, gripped players stay attached as well — no player “Grip” spell exists; it is always an encounter effect.
  • Assign a roll caller. Healers plan for Fiery Grip damage during rolls; the raid should never be surprised by weight shifts during Nuclear Blast setup.
⚠
Callouts: Use clear voice comms (“stack hole”, “roll in 3”, “move amalg to plate”, “nuclear out”) — avoid misleading terms like “press grip”; new players should be taught Fiery Grip + Barrel Roll physics instead.

Opening a plate (Nuclear Blast loop) [N/H]

  • Kill Corrupted Blood on purpose so they drop Residue where the tank can kite the Hideous Amalgamation through it. Kite until 9 Absorbed Blood.
  • Drag the amalgamation flush to the armor plate you are working on; kill it so its Nuclear Blast detonation hits the plate (everyone else runs out of the telegraphed ground circle — it hits players for massive Fire damage).
  • When the plate tears, Burning Tendons appear — pop bloodlust/heroism if scheduled and burn inside the short exposure. If DPS is low, plan externals and pot timings per plate.

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

Heroic increases add and tendon health and layers Blood Corruption management on top of the same three-plate structure. Rolls, Fiery Grip, and Nuclear Blast hurt more — cooldown planning is stricter.

Blood Corruption: Death / Earth (dispel loop)
  • Players periodically receive Blood Corruption: Death and Blood Corruption: Earth. These debuffs are the heroic heartbeat of the encounter — mishandled dispels or timeouts wipe the raid.
  • Death — When its timer runs out, it explodes for lethal raid-wide Shadow damage. Dispelling Death causes it to jump to another player (often random). The goal is to move Death through players in a controlled order so it never expires.
  • Earth — Dispelling Earth also causes a jump. When Earth expires naturally it does a smaller Nature burst around the victim (position those players safely). Advanced groups use Death and Earth interactions (including stacking rules documented for Cataclysm Classic) to “consume” dangerous Death timers — assign two dispellers minimum and a written rotation.
  • Dispel cadence: Never dispel blindly during a Barrel Roll call, during Fiery Grip emergencies, or as the Hideous Amalgamation is lining up Nuclear Blast. Use voice: “Death to Ranged 3,” then confirm Earth holders, etc.
  • Tip: WeakAuras for debuff timers are mandatory. If a Death holder dies mid-route, stop and rebuild the dispel chain before continuing the plate.
⚠
Heroic tip: Roll caller + dispel caller are two different jobs. Practice Amalgamation placement, then layer dispels. Save DPS cooldowns for tendon burn windows only when the Blood Corruption loop is stable.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Main-tank the Hideous Amalgamation; kite through Residue to 9 Absorbed Blood, then anchor on the plate for Nuclear Blast.
  • Round up Corrupted Blood as they spawn; coordinate when to kill them for pool placement.
  • Stack with the raid for Barrel Roll discipline — never alone on the wrong half of the spine.
⚔ DPS
  • Break Fiery Grip with precise bursts; kill Corrupted Blood when the raid needs Residue density.
  • When Burning Tendons spawn, hit nothing else until the tendon dies.
  • Run out of Nuclear Blast; line up cooldowns for tendon burn.
💉 Healers
  • Power through Searing Plasma healing absorbs; heal Fiery Grip victims and Nuclear Blast fallout.
  • Coordinate raid CDs for scripted Barrel Rolls when damage spikes.
  • [H] Track Blood Corruption: Death / Earth; dispel only on call.
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use during a tendon burn phase when the tendon is exposed. All DPS on the tendon; maximize damage in the short window. Can use once per plate if the encounter allows.
