Overview

Hagara the Stormbinder is the fourth boss of Dragon Soul. The fight alternates between a main phase (Ice Tombs, Focused Assault) and two intermission phases: Lightning (conductors / chain lightning) and Ice / Crystal (shield + crystals). Ice Lance exists on both difficulties; on Heroic the bolts hit much harder, so ranged players soak lines together (e.g. Shadow Priest takes six bolts, Warlock soaks the rest) instead of relying on interrupts alone. Heroic Ice intermission adds Frostflake (slow → root → dispel leaves a frost pool) and requires stacking on one crystal for damage. Lightning Heroic uses either two pairs running the ring with everyone mid, or a star pattern with fixed spots per player to instantly charge conductors.

⚠ Enrage
  • 6-minute enrage. Hagara gains Fury. Kill before enrage.
  • Main phase lasts ~50 seconds, then Hagara enters an intermission: either Lightning (chain lightning — spread) or Ice / Crystal (shield, destroy crystals). After each intermission she takes increased damage for 15 seconds — good burn window.
  • Focused Assault: tank swap or mitigate; Ice Tombs must be broken quickly every time.

Phases & Mechanics

Main phase — Ice Tombs, Ice Lance & Focused Assault [N/H]

  • Ice Tomb: Hagara freezes one or more players in an ice block. The trapped player takes damage over time and cannot act. The tomb is a destructible object — the raid must switch to it and break it to free the player before they die.
  • Ice Lance [N/H]: Hagara fires lances down cardinal lines. [N] Damage is lower but still lethal if ignored — ranged should soak with a partner when possible. [H] Bolts hit much harder: assign soakers per line (e.g. SP soaks six bolts, Warlock covers the rest). The second Ice Lance intermission of the fight can spawn only two lines instead of three — adjust positions when the raid leader calls it.
  • Assign 1–2 DPS (or all if needed) to swap to tombs as soon as they appear. Healers keep the trapped player alive while the tomb is being broken.
  • Focused Assault: Hagara focuses on the tank or a target with a heavy attack (frontal cleave or single-target). Tank swap if it stacks, or use cooldowns. Face her away from the raid.

Lightning phase (intermission) [N/H]

  • Hagara becomes immune and channels lightning mechanics (conductors, chained bolts). [N] Basic approach: spread so chains break or bounce safely; heal chip damage.
  • [H] Two common setups: (1) Two groups of two players run opposite directions on the ring while everyone else stays mid — pairs touch each conductor in sequence. (2) Star pattern — every player has a fixed clock spot so the raid instantly closes every lightning pillar without improvising mid-cast.
  • When the channel ends, Hagara returns to the main phase and takes increased damage for 15 seconds.
⚠
Lightning: [N] Spread assignments still help. [H] Pick star or duo-runner strat before pull — mixing them mid-fight wipes the raid.

Ice / Crystal phase (intermission) [N/H]

  • Hagara gains a shield and is immune. Crystals spawn around the room. The raid must destroy the crystals to break her shield; until then she cannot be damaged.
  • [H] Frostflake (Snowflake): random players are slowed until rooted; when dispelled they drop a large frost pool that slows everyone. Dispel only inside Alexstrasza’s bubble at the center so the bad ground stays off the run path. Druids (shapeshift), Paladins (Freedom), Rogues (Cloak) can self-clear — still do it in the bubble because the pool remains either way.
  • [H] Stack the entire raid on one shield crystal while breaking the Frost pillars — HC tuning expects stacked cleave/AoE to beat the shield timer.
  • All DPS switch to crystals. Once the shield is down, resume DPS on Hagara. For 15 seconds after the intermission she takes increased damage — use this window to burn.
⚠
Burn window: After each intermission (Lightning or Ice/Crystal), Hagara takes increased damage for 15 seconds. Save cooldowns for these windows if the fight is tight.
♔

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

🔭 Heroic emphasis (Ice Lance + Frostflake + pillars)
  • Ice Lance is not a Heroic-only mechanic — it is simply mandatory to soak/share on Heroic because each bolt deals far more damage. Plan ranged pairs or trios per line before the pull.
  • Frostflake dispels must happen inside the center bubble; never dispel on the lane. Self-dispels still drop the pool — still use the bubble.
  • Lightning + Ice intermissions both gain tighter positioning checks; use the 15s burn windows after each intermission to make up for lost uptime.
⚠
Heroic tip: Ice Tombs + Ice Lance + Frostflake can overwhelm healers. Break tombs fast, soak lances with assignments (not prayer), and call the two-line Lance phase when it happens.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Tank Hagara; face away from raid
  • Mitigate Focused Assault; swap if stacking
  • [H] Help call Ice Lance for interrupt
⚔ DPS
  • Switch to Ice Tombs immediately when they appear
  • Lightning phase: spread out; Crystal phase: nuke crystals
  • [H] Interrupt Ice Lance when assigned
💉 Healers
  • Heal tomb targets while tombs are being broken
  • Heal through Lightning phase chain damage
  • [H] Top Ice Lance target; use CDs if interrupt missed
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use during main phase when no tombs are up for maximum boss damage, or during Crystal phase to break the shield quickly. Avoid using during Lightning phase (raid is spread and may not be DPSing the boss).
