Overview

Yor'sahj the Unsleeping is the third boss of Dragon Soul. Colored oozes spawn from the sides and move toward him: [N] three oozes per wave, [H] four oozes per wave. If an ooze reaches Yor'sahj, he absorbs that color and gains its ability. The raid must kill the right oozes and let the rest reach so he doesn't get lethal combos. Default kill priority: Yellow (Glowing Blood — huge Void Bolt / attack-speed pressure) > Green (Digestive Acid — raid must spread) > Purple (Deep Corruption — dangerous at 5 stacks, but the mechanic is manageable; Purple is not an automatic first kill). If Red is in the wave, you may kill Green before Yellow if the raid is confident spreading at close range for Yellow — not mandatory. If neither Yellow nor Green is in the wave, you can skip killing oozes entirely or only kill what you need — Purple alone is often fine to leave. Void Bolt is the main tank/raid damage.

⚠
Yellow & Deep Corruption (Purple) [N/H] — If Yellow reaches the boss, Void Bolt and raid damage spike. If Purple reaches the boss, Deep Corruption stacks on the raid; at 5 stacks the raid detonates (wipe). Plan each wave: usually Yellow first, Green second, Purple last unless the wave composition calls for an exception (see below).
Single phase
100% → 0%
Repeating cycle: 3 [N] or 4 [H] oozes spawn → raid kills in priority order (Yellow → Green → Purple; exceptions: Y+G+R may kill Green first; no Y/G → Purple optional) → Yor'sahj gains abilities from colors that reached him. Manage Void Bolt and any absorbed effects. Repeat until dead.

⚠ Enrage
  • 6-minute enrage. Yor'sahj gains Fury. Kill before enrage.
  • Oozes spawn in waves: 3 [N] or 4 [H] per wave. Default: kill Yellow, then Green, then Purple. Phases & Mechanics

Slime colors & kill order [N/H]

  • Oozes per wave: [N] 3 oozes; [H] 4 oozes. Each wave has a random set of colors from: Purple, Yellow, Green, Red, Blue, Black.
  • If an ooze reaches Yor'sahj, he absorbs it and gains that ability. If the raid kills it before it reaches him, he does not gain that color.
  • Yellow — Glowing Blood: 50% attack speed, stronger Void Bolt pressure, double ability refresh. Kill first in most waves (highest raid damage if absorbed).
  • Green — Digestive Acid: each player emits an AoE; raid must spread. Kill second unless you are using the Y+G+R exception below.
  • Purple — Deep Corruption: if it reaches the boss, stacks build on the raid; at 5 stacks the raid detonates (wipe). Purple is a simple mechanic compared to Yellow/Green — kill last in the default order, or skip oozes entirely when there is no Yellow and no Green in the wave.
  • Red — Searing Blood: fire damage. Often left to reach boss. With Yellow + Green + Red together, kill Green first (cannot group for Yellow and spread for Green at once). With Red present, some groups focus Green instead of Yellow first if they can still handle Yellow’s pressure — optional.
  • Blue / Black — Mana Void, adds, or other effects. Priority depends on comp and what you can afford to let through.
⚠
Standard order: Yellow → Green → Purple (then Red/Blue/Black per strategy). Exception: Y+G+R → Green first, then Yellow. Assign DPS to each ooze by color before the pull.

Deep Corruption [N/H]

  • Deep Corruption comes from the Purple ooze if it reaches the boss. Stacks build from healing (and related effects) on your version of the fight — at 5 stacks the raid detonates (wipe).
  • If Purple is absorbed, manage stacks with healing discipline and cooldowns; do not let the raid reach 5 stacks. That does not automatically mean Purple must die first every wave.

Void Bolt [N/H]

  • Yor'sahj casts Void Bolt: heavy Shadow damage on the tank or a target. Main tank damage source.
  • If Yellow ooze is absorbed, Void Bolt hits harder or more often. Healers must keep the tank topped; use cooldowns during high Void Bolt phases.
♔

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

Heroic adds a fourth ooze each wave and increases damage. Kill priority stays Yellow > Green > Purple by default; execution must be cleaner. Deep Corruption and Void Bolt punish mistakes harder — more healing and cooldown coordination required.

⚠
Heroic tip: Assign one caller for Yellow / Green / Purple / Blue each wave. DPS must switch quickly so the wrong ooze does not reach the boss. Healers watch Deep Corruption stacks and Void Bolt spikes when Yellow gets through.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Tank Yor'sahj; face away from raid
  • Void Bolt is main tank damage — use CDs if Yellow absorbed
  • Don't stand in acid (if Black absorbed)
⚔ DPS
  • Kill assigned oozes each wave (Yellow, Green, Purple, Blue per wave plan)
  • Switch to boss when oozes are dead or absorbed
  • Avoid standing in pools; manage Deep Corruption stacks
💉 Healers
  • Heal through Void Bolt and Deep Corruption
  • Dispel if Deep Corruption is dispelable
  • Conserve mana if Blue is killed (Mana Void) or plan mana for Blue reaching the boss
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use after the first wave when the boss has absorbed one or two manageable colors and the raid is stable. Or save for the final 20% when ooze waves are still spawning.
