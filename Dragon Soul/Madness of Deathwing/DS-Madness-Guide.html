Overview

Madness of Deathwing is the final boss of Dragon Soul. The fight takes place on four platforms. On each platform, damage Deathwing's limb (mutated corruption); at 70% and 40% of the limb's HP, Blistering Tentacles spawn and must be killed. On the Alexstrasza (red / left) platform after her buff is active, Blisterings must be killed with direct single-target damage — spreading DoTs and passive multidot/cleave no longer works on them. Survive Elementium Bolt with cooldowns. After four limbs are dealt with, the final phase targets Deathwing's head: Aspects grant Aspect buffs for the burn. [H] adds Corrupting Parasite and other changes (see Heroic section).

Phases & Mechanics

Four platforms & limb phases [N/H]

  • The raid is on one of four platforms. Each platform has a mutated corruption (limb) that must be damaged. At 70% and 40% of the limb's HP, Blistering Tentacles spawn — kill them or they overwhelm the raid with AoE.
  • Alexstrasza's platform (red / full left): once her buff is active, Blistering Tentacles only take meaningful damage from direct single-target attacks — DoT spreading, cleave, and multidot tricks do not work. Assign focused ST on each tentacle.
  • Alexstrasza's Cauterize and other Aspect buffs per platform — use them as intended for healing or damage on that wing. When the limb is destroyed, move to the next platform. Repeat for all four; then the final phase.

Elementium Bolt [N/H]

  • Elementium Bolt is a major ability: Deathwing fires a bolt that deals very high damage to the raid or a single target. It can be lethal if not mitigated.
  • Use raid cooldowns (e.g. Barrier, Rallying Cry, Spirit Link) and personal defensives when Elementium Bolt is cast. Healers should top the raid before each bolt.
⚠
Bolt timing: Assign a caller to announce "Elementium Bolt — CDs" so tanks and healers use cooldowns together. One missed CD rotation can cause multiple deaths.

Final phase & Aspect buffs [N/H]

  • After the four limb phases, Deathwing's head becomes the target. The Dragon Aspects grant Aspect buffs: increased damage, healing, or damage reduction.
  • [N] The head phase begins around 20% of Deathwing's remaining health. [H] The head phase begins at 25% instead of 20% — slightly longer burn.
  • The raid burns Deathwing with these buffs active. Use Lust/Heroism and all cooldowns here. [H] Every 5% of the head's health, Elementium Fragments (bloods) spawn at random spots on the platform — they must be killed with AoE/cleave before they reach the head or they empower Deathwing.
♔

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

Corrupting Parasite [H]
  • Twice per platform, a player gets Corrupting Parasite (debuff with a countdown). When it expires, it explodes — players must move away from that player. A Parasite add then spawns on the ground; it begins casting again — kill it before that cast finishes or it wipes the raid.
  • Until Nozdormu's platform is cleared: the parasitized player should enter Nozdormu's yellow bubble (Time Zone) so the parasite add spawns inside — the bubble slows its cast, buying time to kill it.
  • On the last platform (before the head): the whole raid stacks on the parasite when it spawns, uses defensive cooldowns, and bursts it down ASAP. Parasite is the main heroic wipe mechanic — prioritize it over padding boss DPS.

Heroic increases limb, tentacle, and boss HP; Elementium Bolt and add damage are higher. Head phase at 25% with bloods every 5%. Execution on each platform must be clean so the raid reaches the head with cooldowns and lives.

⚠
Heroic tip: Plan CD rotation for Elementium Bolt on each platform. Save Lust for the final head burn if your timing allows. Parasite > Blisterings > limb DPS when in doubt. On the head, assign AoE/stuns for every blood wave.

Strategy Summary

Role Assignments [N/H]

🛡 Tanks
  • Tank Blistering Tentacles or adds on each platform
  • Help position for Elementium Bolt (soak or mitigate)
  • Final phase: tank any remaining adds; face away from raid
⚔ DPS
  • Kill Blistering Tentacles at 70%/40% limb HP; red platform: direct ST only
  • [H] Kill Corrupting Parasite adds immediately; use Time Zone until Nozdormu wing is done
  • DPS the limb; final phase: burn head, AoE bloods every 5% [H]
💉 Healers
  • Heal through Elementium Bolt; use raid CDs when called
  • Top raid before each bolt; dispel if needed
  • Final phase: maximize healing with Aspect buffs
⚠
Lust / Heroism: Use in the final phase when Aspect buffs are active and the raid is burning Deathwing's head. This gives the maximum benefit. Avoid using on an early platform unless the raid is behind and needs to catch up.
