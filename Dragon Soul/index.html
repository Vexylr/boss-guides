Boss guides (encounter order)

1. Morchok

Stone giant. Stack near boss for Stomp; 3 soakers run to Resonating Crystal. Black Blood: hide behind spikes. [H] Kohcrom split. Tank swap Crush Armor.

View guide →

2. Warlord Zon'ozz

Void orb ping-pong; don’t wall. Psychic Drain on tank. Let orb hit boss for Phase 2. [H] Disrupting Shadows dispel cadence + final bounce counts (7-7-5 style).

View guide →

3. Yor'sahj the Unsleeping

3 [N] or 4 [H] oozes per wave. Priority: Yellow > Green > Purple (Purple optional if no Y/G). Y+G+R → Green first. Void Bolt, Deep Corruption.

View guide →

4. Hagara the Stormbinder

Lightning: spread basics; [H] duo runners or star pattern. Ice: [H] Frostflake bubble dispels + pillar stack. Ice Lance soak [N/H], harder on HC.

View guide →

5. Ultraxion

Hour of Twilight + Heroic Will. Solo-tank meta; Fading Light doesn’t stack — mind HW threat drop. Some servers tune ~124M HP (10H). 6 min burn.

View guide →

6. Warmaster Blackhorn

[H] Goriona lands (90%/40%), deck fire + firefighters, healing absorb, no player extinguish. [N] classic ship/drake flow. Phase 2: Blackhorn + Onslaught.

View guide →

7. Spine of Deathwing

Three armor plates: soak Residue to 9 Absorbed Blood on a Hideous Amalgamation, Nuclear Blast to expose Burning Tendons, burn tendon. Barrel Roll weight + Fiery Grip / pits (no player grip spell). [H] Blood Corruption Death/Earth dispels.

View guide →

8. Madness of Deathwing

Four platforms. Blisterings at 70%/40% limb; red wing ST only. [H] Parasite + head bloods. Elementium Bolt, Cauterize. Final burn + Aspects.

View guide →
