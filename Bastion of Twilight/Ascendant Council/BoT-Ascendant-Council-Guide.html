Overview

The Ascendant Council is a three-phase encounter. Phase 1: Feludius (Water) and Ignacious (Fire). Phase 2: Arion (Lightning) and Terrastra (Earth). Phase 3: all four merge into the Elementium Monstrosity. Elemental counters apply: Fire removes Waterlogged (touch fire), Frost removes Burning Blood (Heart of Ice spread then dispel). Phase 2: you need Grounded (from Gravity Well) to survive Thundershock, and Swirling Winds (from Call Winds cyclone) to survive Quake â€” you cannot have both buffs at once, so alternate based on which ability is next.
