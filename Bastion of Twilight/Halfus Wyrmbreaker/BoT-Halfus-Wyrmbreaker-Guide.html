Overview

Halfus Wyrmbreaker is the first boss in the Bastion of Twilight. He is accompanied by his Proto-Behemoth (flying above) and three of five possible drakes (Normal) or all five drakes (Heroic), chained to the ground. While chained, each drake grants Halfus or the Proto-Behemoth a powerful ability. Releasing drakes turns them hostile but gives Halfus a debuff and, when killed, Dragon's Vengeance â€” +100% damage taken per stack. The fight is about choosing which drakes to release and in what order, then managing tank swaps, interrupts, and raid cooldowns.

Strategy Summary

  • Pull: Release 2â€“4 drakes (at least Storm Rider + Nether Scion). Use immunities (Divine Shield, etc.) to drop Malevolent Strikes on tanks during the chaotic opener.
  • DPS: Kill released drakes quickly. Don't focus only on Orphaned Emerald Whelps â€” let them die to cleave. Always interrupt Shadow Nova.
  • Tanks: Tank swap on Malevolent Strikes. Off-tank picks up released drakes. Damage is highest at the start; use personal defensives.
  • Healers: Rotate cooldowns for Fireball Barrage and Scorching Breath. Save a strong raid CD for Furious Roar (50%) and for the following Shadow Nova recovery.
  • 50%: Before Furious Roar, stack raid, use raid CD. Assign one player (e.g. Mage with Blink) to break stun and interrupt Shadow Nova immediately after Roar.
