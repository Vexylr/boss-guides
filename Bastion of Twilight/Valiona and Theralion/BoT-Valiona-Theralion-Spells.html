Blackout

Blackout (Valiona, ground): hits a random player with a healing absorb. When it expires or is dispelled, it explodes for heavy Shadow damage split among everyone within a few yards. Stack the raid on the target (usually behind the boss), then dispel. Immunities (Cloak, Ice Block, etc.) can remove it safely alone.

Wowhead — spell #86788 (tooltip reference)

Devouring Flames

Devouring Flames (Valiona, ground): channeled ~180° cone toward a random direction. Damage ticks every second and falls off with distance. If you are close, run through/behind Valiona; if you are ranged and she turns on you, sprint to maximum range.

Wowhead — spell #86840 (tooltip reference)

Dazzling Destruction

Dazzling Destruction (Theralion, air): purple swirls on the ground after a short cast. Standing in the impact sends you to the Twilight Realm. Avoid the zones; players already in Twilight die if hit.

Wowhead — spell #86408 (tooltip reference)

Deep Breath

Deep Breath (Valiona, air before landing): covers a large wedge of the room with Twilight flame. Getting hit ports you to the Twilight Realm (or kills if you were already there). Watch her facing and sidestep the breath.

Wowhead — spell #86014 (tooltip reference)

Twilight Shift

Twilight Shift [H]: stacking tank debuff from melee. At five stacks it triggers Shifting Reality on nearby players—keep the tank away from the raid unless you intentionally send people in.

Wowhead — spell #93057 (tooltip reference)

Twilight Zone

Twilight Zone: Shadow DoT that stacks while you are in the Twilight Realm (+Shadow damage taken per stack). Leave quickly via the portal; Rogues/Mages can clear stacks with Cloak or Ice Block.

Wowhead — spell #86628 (tooltip reference)

Shifting Reality

Shifting Reality [H]: detonation around the tank when Twilight Shift stacks expire; damages nearby allies and sends them to the Twilight Realm. Used to send a controlled group to kill Twilight Sentries.

Wowhead — spell #93055 (tooltip reference)

Twilight Meteorite

Twilight Meteorite (Valiona, air): marks a player; after a few seconds it deals split Shadow damage to the target and anyone stacked nearby. Run to melee (or a pre-planned clump) so the raid shares the hit.

Wowhead — spell #86013 (tooltip reference)

Engulfing Magic

Engulfing Magic (Theralion, ground): debuff that makes you pulse AoE damage to allies. Run more than ~10 yards from the raid until it expires.

Wowhead — spell #86631 (tooltip reference)

Fabulous Flames

Fabulous Flames (Theralion, ground): large fire pool on a random ranged spot. Move as a group to stack pools and save space. Standing in fire too long pulls you to the Twilight Realm.

Wowhead — spell #86506 (tooltip reference)
