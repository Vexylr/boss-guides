Overview

Valiona and Theralion are twin dragons with a shared health pool. One is always on the ground, the other airborne; they swap periodically. Start: Valiona grounded, Theralion in the air. Damage to either counts toward the same HP. Ground / Air & Twilight Realm

  • One dragon is always ground, one air. They alternate: after Valiona does three Deep Breaths she lands and Theralion flies up (and vice versa).
  • Deep Breath (Valiona, before landing): covers ~1/3 of room in Twilight Flames. Hit = sent to Twilight Realm. Anyone already in Twilight Realm hit by it dies. Track her position and move out of path.
  • Dazzling Destruction (Theralion, from air): purple ground swirls. Stand in them = sent to Twilight Realm. Avoid.
  • Twilight Realm: Dodge floating orbs (Unstable Twilight). Find portal to return. [H] Twilight Sentries spawn; send healer + DPS in to clear. Twilight Zone stacks (Shadow DoT, +10% Shadow damage taken per stack). Rogues/Mages can clear with Cloak / Ice Block.
  • [H] Twilight Shift on tank: at 5 stacks, Shifting Reality â€” damages nearby and sends them to Twilight. Tank stand alone; assigned players move to tank at 4 stacks if they want to be sent in to kill Sentries.

Strategy Summary

  • Positioning: Keep bosses near middle. Spread when Valiona ground (Twilight Blast); stack when Theralion ground (except Engulfing Magic targets).
  • Blackout: Always stack before dispel. Use raid CD when soaking Blackout or Twilight Meteorite if needed.
  • Heroic: Tank swap for Twilight Shift; send dedicated players into Twilight Realm to kill Sentries. Use disorients/knockbacks/incapacitates on Spitecallers (no stun/interrupt or they get Indomitable).
  • Enrage: 10 minutes. Burn both dragons; they share HP so focus fire is fine.
