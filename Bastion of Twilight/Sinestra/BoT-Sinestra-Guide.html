Overview

Sinestra is a Heroic-only boss. She starts at 60% HP and is Drained (40% reduced damage). Phase 1: manage Wrack (dispel chain — first target soaks as long as possible with personals so later bounces have shorter duration), Twilight Slicer (two players kite orbs to the back), and Twilight Whelps (tank them, don't kill yet). Goal: reach 30% before third Whelp wave and before a second Wrack. Phase 2: Calen shields raid from Twilight Extinction; kill Whelps in shield, then burn Sinestra's Mana Barrier and destroy both Pulsing Twilight Eggs when carapace drops. Phase 3: full-HP Sinestra, no Drained; burn with Essence of the Red. Manage Whelps (kill one at a time at the back to delay revives), tank swap Twilight Spit, CDs for Flame Breath.

Phase 1 (60% → 30%)

  • Wrack — ramping Shadow DoT. Dispelling bounces to 2 nearby; each bounce resets ramp but same duration. First target: use all personals, soak as long as possible, then dispel so subsequent Wracks are shorter. Then dispel every 10–15s to limit bounces.
  • Twilight Slicer — 2 players fixated by orbs connected by a beam (high damage in beam and near orbs). Kite to the back, U-turn along wall, return. Raid stay clear of orbs and beam.
  • Twilight Whelps — 5 per wave. Second tank picks up, bring near Sinestra for cleave. Do not kill; they drop Twilight Essence pools that grow and revive dead Whelps. You'll kill them in Phase 2 inside Calen's shield.
  • Flame Breath — every ~20s; manageable while Drained. Use personals if needed.
⚠
Benchmark: reach 30% before the third set of Whelps and before a second Wrack. That keeps Phase 2 manageable.
✦

Phase 2 (Calen & Eggs)

  • Calen appears, places shield at back. Entire raid stack in shield for Twilight Extinction or wipe.
  • Tank with Whelps brings them to back of shield. Raid AoE Whelps in one spot → all pools in one place. Whelps revive; drag out of pools, AoE again — they die without dropping new pools and despawn.
  • Sinestra has Mana Barrier (takes mana instead of HP). Heal Calen — he uses Pyrrhic Focus (burns his HP for mana). Most healers on Calen.
  • Ranged DPS burn Sinestra to drain mana. When low, she removes Twilight Carapace from both Pulsing Twilight Eggs for 30s. Split ranged evenly, kill both eggs in that window. If you don't, drain her mana again for another carapace drop.
  • Twilight Spitecaller — spawns; casts Unleash Essence (10% max HP/s). Do NOT stun or normal interrupt — they get Indomitable and wipe. Use disorients, knockbacks, incapacitates (Death Grip, Gouge, Repentance, etc.).
  • Twilight Drake — tank it, face away (Twilight Breath cone).
✦

Phase 3 (100% → 0%)

  • Sinestra at full HP, no Drained. Calen dies but gives raid Essence of the Red. Wrack, Twilight Slicer, Twilight Whelps all return at full damage.
  • Kill remaining Drakes/Spitecallers, return to P1 positions. Pop all DPS cooldowns with Essence of the Red and burn.
  • Twilight Whelps: AoE to low HP, tank drags to back. Kill one at a time; tank moves away after each so pool doesn't revive others immediately. Delays room coverage by Twilight Essence.
  • Tank swap for Twilight Spit (from Whelps); or Paladin Divine Protection to reset.
  • Flame Breath hits hard. Rotate raid and healer CDs. Single-target CDs on Whelp tank.
