Overview

Cho'gall alternates between Flame's Orders and Shadow's Orders. The entire raid manages Corrupted Blood: taking damage from his abilities increases corruption. At 25/50/75/100 you get increasingly severe debuffs; at 100% you become unhealable but deal double damage. Phase 1: avoid Blaze, interrupt Conversion, tank Corrupting Adherents away and interrupt Depravity; kill Shadow Lord on Heroic. Phase 2 at 25%: burn Cho'gall while handling Darkened Creations and Spiked Tentacles; Corruption of the Old God ramps with your corruption level.

Phase 1
100% → 25%
Flame's Orders: Blaze patches, Flaming Destruction. Shadow's Orders: Empowered Shadows (raid AoE). Tank swap Fury of Cho'gall. Corrupting Adherents: tank away, interrupt Depravity, move from Corrupting Crash. Fester Blood: kill oozes, kill Adherents before Fester.

25% → 0%
True form. Corruption of the Old God on everyone (+3% damage per corruption stack). Interrupt and kill Darkened Creations (Debilitating Beam). Avoid Spiked Tentacles. Save DPS CDs and rotate raid/healer defensives.

Corrupted Blood

Corruption increases from boss/add damage. Each stack adds 3% damage from Corruption of the Old God. Thresholds:

  • 25: Corruption: Accelerated — dispellable DoT that raises corruption if not removed.
  • 50: Corruption: Sickness — periodic vomit (Shadow damage in front). Face away from raid.
  • 75: Corruption: Malformation — Shadow Bolts at nearby allies. Move away from raid.
  • 100: Corruption: Absolute — unhealable, but double damage and instant casts. DPS race to kill before death.

Sources: Corruption of the Old God (1/tick), Festering Blood (5), Blood of the Old God (2/melee), Spilled Blood pool (5/s), Debilitating Beam (2/s), Corrupting Crash (10), Depravity (10), etc.

Phase 1 Mechanics

Cho'gall

  • Fury of Cho'gall — tank debuff, +20% Shadow/Physical taken per stack. Tank swap every 1–2 stacks.
  • Conversion — mind controls players to buff him (Twisted Devotion, +5% damage per stack). Interrupt or CC to break.
  • Flame's Orders: Flaming Destruction (Fire on auto), Blaze at random spots — avoid. [H] Fire Elemental spawns first; can cleave but focus Shadow Lord.
  • Shadow's Orders: Empowered Shadows — high Shadow damage every 3s. Rotate raid CDs. [H] Shadow Lord spawns; DPS it as low as possible before Cho'gall absorbs it (each 10% HP left = extra Empowered stack).

Corrupting Adherents

  • Off-tank picks up (1–2 on Heroic). Tank away from raid but in healer range.
  • Depravity — must interrupt. Massive Shadow damage + 10 corruption to nearby.
  • Corrupting Crash — projectile at random location. Move out; high damage + 10 corruption.
  • When they die, Spilled Blood of the Old God pool remains. Don't kill them on top of raid. 30s after spawn, Fester Blood: Adherents get Festering Blood (heavy AoE); pools spawn Blood of the Old God oozes. Slow and AoE oozes; kill Adherents before or during Fester.
✦

Phase 2 (25% → 0%)

  • Cho'gall absorbs remaining Adherents and pools. Tank swap still for Fury.
  • Darkened Creation — interrupt and kill (Debilitating Beam on random player).
  • Spiked Tentacle — avoid; don't waste DPS (too much HP).
  • Corruption of the Old God — constant raid damage; +3% per corruption stack. Save DPS cooldowns, rotate defensives and healer CDs. Push before corruption and damage overwhelm.
