Overview

Chimaeron is a two-headed beast. For most of the fight the raid is kept at 1 HP by Finkle's mixture (NPC Finkle Einhorn provides a buff that prevents death below a certain HP). Double Attack forces tank swaps. Periodically Chimaeron enters Feud — the two heads feud and he casts Massacre, dealing lethal damage that is split among all players in each group. The raid must split into two groups (e.g. one per head); each group soaks one Massacre. If one group has too few people they die. Healers must top the raid between Feuds so Finkle's mixture can keep people at 1 HP. Breakout phase: when Chimaeron reaches low HP he stops using the 1-HP mechanic and must be burned quickly.

⚡

Feud & Massacre

  • Feud — Chimaeron's two heads feud; he becomes immune and casts Massacre twice (one per "head" / group).
  • Massacre — deals a large amount of damage split among all players in the affected group. You must have two groups; each Massacre hits one group. If a group has too few people the damage per player is lethal. Split raid evenly (e.g. 5 and 5 in 10-man).
  • Before Feud, healers top the raid so everyone survives the split damage. After Massacre, everyone will be at 1 HP (Finkle's mixture); healers top again before next Feud or any other damage.
⚡

Tank & Healer Notes

  • Double Attack — stacking debuff on tank; tank swap so stacks don't get too high.
  • Finkle's mixture keeps players from dying below 1 HP; any hit after that will kill. Healers must keep raid topped between Feuds and avoid letting anyone take a killing blow at 1 HP (e.g. from Caustic Slime or boss melee on wrong target).
  • Low HP / breakout: at a certain HP threshold Chimaeron stops applying the 1-HP mechanic and the raid can be healed normally; burn him before enrage or mechanics overwhelm.
