Overview

Maloriak drinks a vial from his cauldron every 40s: Red, Blue, or Green (order differs; Normal: Red/Blue then Green; [H] starts with Dark Magic). You must complete two full cycles (so all 18 Aberrations are released and killed during Green phases) before pushing to 25%. Interrupt Arcane Storm always; purge or Spellsteal Remedy. Release Aberrations spawns 3 per side (6 total); interrupt it if you already have enough adds. Aberrations have Growth Catalyst (stacking buff); keep them away from Maloriak and kite (e.g. Hunter Ice Trap). Kill all 9 Aberrations during each Debilitating Slime (Green phase). At 25%, Phase 2: Release All Minions frees any remaining Aberrations + 2 Prime Subjects. Tank Prime Subjects separately; burn Maloriak. 6-minute enrage.
