Overview

Four golems share one health pool: Arcanotron, Electron, Magmatron, Toxitron. Only one is active at a time (random start). Each has 100 energy that drains in combat. At 50 energy they shield and the next golem activates — you deal with two sets of mechanics until the first hits 0 and goes inactive. Then the second drains from 100 → 50 → 0. Never attack a golem while it has its 50-energy shield (Barrier/Power Conversion/Unstable Shield/Poison Soaked Shell) unless you're purging/Spellstealing Arcanotron stacks. Drag boss into Toxitron's Chemical Cloud for +50% damage taken. Worst combo: Electron + Magmatron (heavy raid damage) — rotate raid CDs.

Golem Abilities

Magmatron

  • Incineration Security Measure — high Fire raid damage over 4s. Raid CD.
  • Acquiring Target → Flamethrower — marked player move away; raid behind boss. Personal CD on target. [H] Encasing Shadows roots target — Freedom or raid move away.
  • At 50 energy: Barrier. Do not break (wipe). Swap to other golem.

Arcanotron

  • Arcane Annihilator — interrupt or ~80k to random player.
  • Power Generator — on random player; stand in for +50% damage and mana. Save DPS CDs for this. [H] Overcharged: +100% damage, field grows and explodes (Arcane Blowback) — leave before explode.
  • At 50 energy: Power Conversion — damage gives Converted Power stacks (+Arcane dmg & haste). Can cleave/DoT if you purge or Mage Spellsteal stacks.

Electron

  • Electrical Discharge — bounces between nearby players, damage increases each bounce. Melee spread; split into groups to limit bounces.
  • Lightning Conductor — random player damages nearby. Move away. [H] Shadow Infusion on same target — raid-wide damage higher when target is far. Stack on target, use raid CD; after 5s becomes Shadow Conductor (damage based on distance from target — stack). Priest Leap of Faith can pull target in.
  • At 50 energy: Unstable Shield — attacking causes massive AoE. Swap off, no cleave.

Toxitron

  • Chemical Bomb → Chemical Cloud — poison puddles, +50% damage taken inside. Tank drag current golem into cloud.
  • Poison Protocol — Poison Bomb oozes fixate; slow and kill (reach target = kill + puddle). [H] Oozes also yank players toward the Chemical Cloud (strong positional pull); spread, slow roots, and kill oozes before the drag stacks casualties.
  • At 50 energy: Poison Soaked Shell — attacking gives Soaked In Poison (Nature DoT, stacks). Swap to other golem; Expunge Poison by attacking other golem (deals extra). One dispel clears full stack.
⚡

Strategy Summary

  • At 50 energy: Current golem shields — drag it away from raid, swap to the newly activated golem. Do not hit Barrier (Magmatron), Unstable Shield (Electron); can hit Arcanotron if purging/Spellsteal; avoid building too many Soaked In Poison on Toxitron.
  • Toxitron cloud: Position so you can pull active golem into cloud for +50% damage.
  • Electron + Magmatron: Hardest combo. Rotate multiple raid defensives and healer CDs.
