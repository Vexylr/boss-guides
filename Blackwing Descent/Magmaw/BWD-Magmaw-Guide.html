Overview

Magmaw is a lava worm that cannot move; he emerges from a lava pool. A tank must always be in melee range or he uses Molten Tantrum (wipe). He uses Magma Spit (random target, heal through), Lava Spew (raid Fire damage every 20s — use raid CD), Pillar of Flame (ranged target, 5s then eruption + Lava Parasites), and every 90s Mangle (tank picked up, heavy damage) followed by Massive Crash. After Massive Crash, two players mount Magmaw and use Launch Hook together to chain him to the pillar for Exposed Head of Magmaw (30s, +100% damage). Tank gets Sweltering Armor (-50% armor 1 min) — off-tank taunts.

Abilities

  • Pillar of Flame — ranged target; 5s later eruption kills anyone in area and spawns Lava Parasites. Move as group away before eruption. Parasites spread Parasitic Infection (Infectious Vomit, more parasites). Kite with Frost DK Chilblains or kill.
  • Lava Spew — every 20s, moderate Fire over 6s. Raid CD.
  • Mangle — every 90s, tank picked up, very high Physical every 2s. Tank keeps attacking and uses defensives; rotate CD on tank. Then Massive Crash — one side of room, move out or die.
  • After Crash: two players click Magmaw to ride. Both use Launch Hook at same time (countdown) to chain to pillar. Success = Exposed Head 30s, tank freed. Fail = tank dies at 30s.
⚡

Chain Phase (Exposed Head)

  • Assign two riders (e.g. two melee). After Massive Crash, both run to Magmaw and click to mount.
  • Vocal countdown (3-2-1) and both use Launch Hook at once. Single use = fail.
  • Exposed Head appears; all DPS burn it (+100% damage). Tank is freed; has Sweltering Armor. Off-tank taunt and tank Magmaw.
⚡

Heroic-Only [H]

  • Blazing Inferno — Nefarius drops meteor on random ranged. Move as group; fire trail leaves patches — avoid.
  • Blazing Bone Construct — spawns after meteor. Off-tank pick up, drag near melee, face away (Fiery Slash cleave). At 20% it casts Armageddon (8s) — kill before finish or wipe.
  • Under 30%: Shadow Breath — Nefarius hits random target + nearby. Spread out. DPS race; save raid CDs. Tip: hold at ~33%, wait for Mangle/Crash, chain again and Bloodlust on Exposed Head to burn last 30%.
