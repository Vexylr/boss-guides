# **FIRELANDS — ALYSRAZOR**
## Boss guide · Normal + Heroic · Discord-ready

**Tag key:** **[N]** = Normal only · **[H]** = Heroic only · **[N/H]** = Both

---

## **OVERVIEW**

Alysrazor is a **four-stage cyclical** encounter. She flies around the arena while players collect **Molten Feathers** (3 = **Wings of Flame**, 30 sec flight) and pass through **rings of fire** to gain **Blazing Power** (up to 25 stacks, haste, refreshes Wings). **Stage 1:** Ground handles **Blazing Talon Initiates** (interrupt **Fieroblast**), **Voracious Hatchlings** (feed **Plump Lava Worms** to prevent Tantrum), and **Herald of the Burning End** (dodge **Molten Meteor**); flyers DPS Alysrazor. **Stage 2:** Wings are removed; raid dodges **Fiery Tornadoes** and stays between **Harsh Winds** (far) and **Fiery Vortex** (center). **Stage 3:** She **crashes (Burnout)** — +50% damage taken; interrupt **Blazing Talon Clawshapers** to delay her re-ignition; burn her. **Stage 4:** She reignites at 50 energy; **Blazing Claw** cleave (tank swap); at 100 energy she knocks back and returns to Stage 1. **15-minute hard enrage.** Heroic: **Firestorm** in air (massive line-of-sight damage); **Incendiary Cloud** always 3; **Unstable Molten** and more damage.

| Phase | Goal |
|--------|------|
| **Stage 1 (air)** | Ground: kill Initiates, feed Hatchlings worms, dodge meteor. Flyers: 3 feathers → Wings, fly through rings (Blazing Power), DPS boss. |
| **Stage 2 (tornadoes)** | Dodge Fiery Tornadoes; stay in safe band (not center, not outer edge). Pass through rings to extend buffs. |
| **Stage 3 (Burnout)** | She lands, +50% damage taken. Interrupt Clawshapers; **Lust** and burn. |
| **Stage 4 (ground burn)** | Tank swap on Blazing Claw; burn before she hits 100 energy and returns to Stage 1. |

- **Enrage:** **15 minutes** [N/H] hard enrage.
- **Molten Feathers** [N/H]: Alysrazor **molts** and drops feathers. **3 feathers** = **Wings of Flame** (fly for **30 sec**). Feathers also give movement speed and cast-while-moving.
- **Blazing Power** [N/H]: **Rings of fire** appear (3 sec). Passing through grants **Blazing Power**: +8% haste per stack, 40 s, stacks to **25**. Each stack **refreshes Wings of Flame** and restores resources. At **24 stacks** you gain **Alysra's Razor** (+75% crit, 40 s). Rings appear in Stage 1 (air) and Stage 2 (ground).

## **STAGE 1 — AIR (FLY & GROUND)** [N/H]

### **Flying**

- Get **3 Molten Feathers** → **Wings of Flame** (30 s). Fly to DPS Alysrazor. Pass through **rings** to stack **Blazing Power** and refresh Wings (stay airborne indefinitely with good ring use).
- **Incendiary Cloud** [N/H]: Patches that deal **50,000 Fire/1.5 s**; avoid. **[H]** She always creates **3** clouds.
- **Blazing Claw** [N/H]: She flies through the center and claws in a cone (**100k Physical**); don't stand in the strafing path. **[H]** In air, **Firestorm** can replace this: after 5 s, **100,000 Fire/s for 10 s** to all in line of sight — avoid or use cooldowns.

### **Ground: adds**

- **Blazing Talon Initiate** [N/H]: **Interrupt Fieroblast** (40k Fire + DoT). If it goes off, the Initiate gains **Fire it Up!** (faster casts); eventually unmanageable. **Brushfire** (moving fire patch, 40k/s in 4 yd) cannot be interrupted. Kill Initiate first.
- **Voracious Hatchling** [N/H]: Two hatch from **Molten Eggs** each cycle, and **Plump Lava Worms** arrive in **two waves** per ground phase — plan tank kiting paths accordingly. **Imprint** always targets the **closest player at the moment the egg breaks** (the hatchling’s “mother”). If the closest player is **not** your ground tank, the hatchling **cannot** be taunted onto the tank until that player dies — have the tank hug each egg as it opens. Tanks deal **+1000% damage** to hatchlings while imprinted, so a misplaced imprint is usually a wipe.
- **Herald of the Burning End** [N/H]: Casts **Cataclysm** (immune until cast). **Molten Meteor** rolls across arena — **500,000 Fire** in 5 yd. Destroy it before the wall or dodge; if it hits the wall it splits into **Molten Boulders** (32k/1.5 s, 3 yd). Assign DPS to kill meteor.

---

## **STAGE 2 — TORNADOES** [N/H]

- After ~5 s, **Wings of Flame** are removed from everyone. Alysrazor flies in a tight circle; **Fiery Vortex** in center (**50,000 Fire/0.5 s** within 15 yd). **Harsh Winds**: **50,000 Fire/s for 5 s** to players **>60 yd** from vortex (stacks). **Fiery Tornadoes** move around — **40,000 Fire/s** within 4 yd.
- **Safe zone:** Between center and outer edge. Dodge tornadoes (change lanes); run through **rings** to extend Blazing Power/Alysra's Razor into Stage 3.
- **[H]** More damage; positioning is critical.

---

## **STAGE 3 — BURNOUT** [N/H]

- Alysrazor **crashes** with 0 Molten Power. **Burnout**: immobilized, **+50% damage taken**. When hit by harmful spells she emits **Essence of the Green** (restores 10% max mana to caster). Phase lasts until she reaches **50 Molten Power** (max ~33 s, often ~20).
- **Blazing Talon Clawshapers** channel **Ignition** (restore 1 Molten Power/s). **Interrupt and stun** to delay her re-ignition (interrupt → stun → interrupt). Tanks position to pick her up for Stage 4.
- **Bloodlust / Heroism / Time Warp** here. All DPS and healers burn her.

---

## **STAGE 4 — GROUND BURN** [N/H]

- At **50 Molten Power** she **reignites**. Tankable. **Blazing Claw**: **100k Physical** in a 25 yd cone every 1.5 s; applies stacking +10% Fire/Physical damage taken (15 s). **Tank swap** so debuff doesn't stack too high. **Blazing Buffet**: **10,000 Fire/s** raid-wide while she's ignited.
- At **100 Molten Power** she knocks everyone back, drops feathers, and returns to **Stage 1**. If you reach a **third** Stage 4, she may fail to fully re-ignite and spam Blazing Buffet/Blazing Claw (soft enrage) — kill before then.

---

## **HEROIC-ONLY MECHANICS** [H]

- **Firestorm** in Stage 1 (air): After 5 s, **100,000 Fire/s for 10 s** to all in line of sight. **Hide behind the rock left by a destroyed Molten Meteor** (the meteor stays where you kill it) to break line of sight — assign a shot-caller for “meteor LOS now.”
- **Incendiary Cloud:** Always **3** clouds (not just periodic).
- **Unstable Molten** [H]: Additional ground hazard; avoid.
- Higher boss and add HP; tighter tornado/positioning; more damage overall.

## **STRATEGY SUMMARY**

**Stage 1** [N/H]
- **Flyers (1–3):** Grab 3 feathers → Wings; fly through rings, DPS Alysrazor. Avoid clouds; **[H]** avoid Firestorm.
- **Ground tank:** Be the **closest player to each egg** as it hatches so you're **Imprinted**; kite Tantrum to **both** worm waves. Don't pick up 3 feathers (you'd fly and lose imprint). Kill Hatchlings (you have +1000% damage to them).
- **Ground DPS:** Kill **Blazing Talon Initiate** first (interrupt Fieroblast). Help with meteor. Avoid Brushfire and Lava Spew.
- **Healers:** One can grab feathers for mobility; heal Hatchling tank and raid. **[H]** Watch Firestorm.

**Stage 2** [N/H]
- **Raid:** Stay in safe band; dodge tornadoes (change lanes). Run through rings to extend Blazing Power into Stage 3.
- **Flyers:** If still in air, hit 1–2 rings to carry buffs into Stage 3.

**Stage 3** [N/H]
- **Tanks:** Interrupt/stun Clawshapers; move to tank position for Stage 4.
- **Raid:** Lust; burn Alysrazor. Stack for AoE heals before Stage 4.

**Stage 4** [N/H]
- **Tanks:** Triangle positioning; swap on Blazing Claw stacks (or single-tank with CDs to ~11–12 stacks).
- **Raid:** Behind boss; burn before 100 energy.
