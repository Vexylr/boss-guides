Overview

Alysrazor is a four-stage cyclical encounter. 3 Molten Feathers = Wings of Flame (30 s flight); pass through rings for Blazing Power (25 stacks, haste, refreshes Wings). Stage 1: Ground handles Initiates (interrupt Fieroblast), Hatchlings (feed worms), Herald (dodge meteor); flyers DPS boss. Stage 2: Dodge tornadoes; stay in safe band. Stage 3: She crashes (Burnout +50% dmg); interrupt Clawshapers; Lust and burn. Stage 4: Blazing Claw tank swap; at 100 energy she returns to Stage 1. 15 min enrage. [H] Firestorm, 3 Incendiary Clouds, Unstable Molten.

StageGoal

2 (tornadoes)Dodge Fiery Tornadoes; stay in safe band; pass through rings to extend buffs.
3 (Burnout)She lands +50% dmg taken. Interrupt Clawshapers; Lust and burn.
4 (ground burn)Tank swap on Blazing Claw; burn before 100 energy (returns to Stage 1).

Stage 1 — Air (Fly & Ground)

Flying [N/H]

  • Get 3 Molten Feathers → Wings of Flame (30 s). Fly through rings to stack Blazing Power and refresh Wings.
  • Incendiary Cloud: 50k Fire/1.5 s; avoid. [H] She always creates 3 clouds.
  • Blazing Claw: She claws through center (100k Physical cone); don't stand in strafing path. [H] Firestorm can replace: 100k Fire/s for 10 s to all in LoS — hide behind the rock from a killed Molten Meteor to break LoS.

Ground: adds [N/H]

  • Blazing Talon Initiate: Interrupt Fieroblast. Brushfire (moving fire) cannot be interrupted. Kill Initiate first.
  • Voracious Hatchling: Two eggs per cycle; worms come in two waves. Imprint snaps to whoever is closest when the egg cracks — tanks must hug eggs or a random player becomes "mom" (not tauntable until death). Tanks get +1000% dmg vs hatchlings; feed Plump Lava Worms to stop Tantrum; avoid Lava Spew.
  • Herald of the Burning End: Molten Meteor (500k Fire in 5 yd). Destroy before wall or dodge; assign DPS.
🔥

Stage 2 — Tornadoes

  • Wings removed after ~5 s. Fiery Vortex in center (50k Fire/0.5 s within 15 yd). Harsh Winds >60 yd from vortex (50k Fire/s, 5 s). Fiery Tornadoes (40k Fire/s within 4 yd).
  • Stay in safe zone (between center and outer edge). Dodge tornadoes; run through rings to extend Blazing Power into Stage 3.
🔥

Stage 3 — Burnout

  • She crashes; Burnout: immobilized, +50% damage taken. Essence of the Green (restores 10% max mana when you hit her). Phase until 50 Molten Power (max ~33 s).
  • Blazing Talon Clawshapers channel Ignition. Interrupt and stun to delay re-ignition. Lust; all DPS burn her.
🔥

Stage 4 — Ground Burn

  • At 50 Molten Power she reignites. Blazing Claw: 100k Physical cone every 1.5 s, stacking +10% dmg taken. Tank swap. Blazing Buffet: 10k Fire/s raid-wide.
  • At 100 Molten Power she knocks back and returns to Stage 1. Third Stage 4 = soft enrage (spam); kill before then.
🔥

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

  • Firestorm in Stage 1 (air): 100k Fire/s for 10 s to all in line of sight — stand behind destroyed Molten Meteor rocks for LOS, plus personal CDs.
  • Incendiary Cloud: Always 3 clouds.
  • Unstable Molten [H]: Additional ground hazard; avoid.
  • Higher boss and add HP; tighter tornado/positioning; more damage overall.
🔥

Strategy Summary

Stage 1: Flyers
  • 3 feathers → Wings; fly through rings, DPS boss; avoid clouds; [H] Firestorm
Stage 1: Ground tank
  • Be closest at egg hatch; kite Tantrum through two worm waves; kill Hatchlings (+1000% dmg)
Stage 1: Ground DPS
  • Kill Initiate (interrupt Fieroblast); help with meteor; avoid Brushfire
Stage 2–4
  • Stage 2: dodge tornadoes, run through rings. Stage 3: interrupt Clawshapers, Lust, burn. Stage 4: tank swap Blazing Claw, burn
