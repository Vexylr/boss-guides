Steering Rhyolith [N/H]

  • Rhyolith has a left and right foot with separate health bars. Damage is equalized between feet over time.
  • More damage on left foot â†’ he turns left. More on right foot â†’ turns right. Equal damage â†’ walks straight.
  • Direction gauge: 0% = hard left, 50% = straight, 100% = hard right. Allow ~1 second for his turn to register.
  • Assign a dedicated Pilot (often melee DPS; ranged usually on adds) to call "left foot" or "right foot" and choose which active volcano to route toward next.

Turning sensitivity [N/H]

  • Clients disagree on whether steering favors damage, hit count, class, or player count on a foot. Use a vocal pilot, put multiple damage sources on the foot you need to turn, and confirm behavior on your realm instead of debating a universal formula.
