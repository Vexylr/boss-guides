Overview

Shannox is fought with his two hounds Riplimb and Rageface. He throws Hurl Spear; Riplimb fetches it (while Shannox has no spear he does less damage). Crystal Prison Traps appear at random, arm after 2 seconds, and can imprison Rageface or Riplimb. Immolation Trap (fire patches) exists on Normal and Heroic. [N] Both dogs must die before Shannox reaches ~30% HP or they enrage and one-shot the raid. [H] Dogs do not need to die — trap them to reset stacks, land a 40k+ crit on Rageface during Face Rage to break it, and mostly DPS the boss (often easier than Normal dog juggling). Jagged Tear still applies to tanks.

Mechanics

Shannox [N/H]

  • Hurl Spear: Throws spear at a location; Riplimb fetches it. When Riplimb returns the spear, Shannox hits harder. Trap Riplimb in Crystal Prisons when the spear is down.
  • Arcing Slash: Cleave that applies Jagged Tear (stacking Physical DoT, ~3 s ticks, ~24–30 s). Tanks use defensives when stacks are high.
  • Tank Shannox away from raid; position so Rageface and Riplimb can be kited over traps.

Riplimb [N/H]

  • Runs to Hurl Spear and brings it back. While trapped (Crystal Prison), he can't fetch. Can be tanked or kited; his attacks also apply Jagged Tear. DPS when safe; trap him so Shannox doesn't get spear.

Rageface [N/H]

  • Face Rage: Fixates on random players (can't be tanked normally). Leaps at them, stunning and dealing heavy Physical damage that increases. Applies Rage stacks.
  • Rage: At 5 stacks Rageface devours the target (death). Use Crystal Prison to imprison him and break fixate, or kill Rageface before 5 stacks.
  • Lure Rageface over an armed trap to lock him; DPS while he's trapped. [H] 40k+ crit during Face Rage frees the victim.

Traps & Jagged Tear [N/H]

  • Crystal Prison: Arms after 2 s. Use to trap Rageface or Riplimb. Don't stand in once armed unless intentional.
  • Immolation Trap [N/H]: Fire on ground; avoid on both difficulties.
  • Jagged Tear: Applied by Shannox (Arcing Slash) and Riplimb. Stacking Physical DoT. Tanks use defensives and coordinate heals when high.
  • Kill order: Rageface first (stop devours) then Riplimb then Shannox; or bring all three low and kill simultaneously to minimize Frenzy.

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

  • Higher boss/add HP. Focus on trap rotations and Face Rage crits, not dog execute HP.
  • Immolation + Crystal placement both matter.

Strategy Summary

Tanks
  • One tank: Shannox (avoid Arcing Slash cleave; manage Jagged Tear)
  • Offtank/kite Rageface over traps or tank Riplimb; trap Rageface so no one gets 5 Rage (devour)
  • Position so Riplimb can be trapped by Crystal Prisons
DPS
  • Priority: Rageface (stop devours) or Riplimb (stop spear), then Shannox; or all three low then simultaneous kill
  • Avoid spear impact; don't stand in Crystal Prison or [H] Immolation Trap once armed
Healers
  • Heal tank damage (Jagged Tear stacks); watch for Rage stacks and Face Rage burst
  • [H] Frenzy phase is intense; more damage overall
