# **FIRELANDS — SHANNOX**
## Boss guide · Normal + Heroic · Discord-ready

**Tag key:** **[N]** = Normal only · **[H]** = Heroic only · **[N/H]** = Both

---

## **OVERVIEW**

Shannox is fought with his two hounds **Riplimb** and **Rageface**. He throws **Hurl Spear**; **Riplimb** fetches it and returns it to Shannox (while Shannox has no spear he does less damage). **Crystal Prison Traps** appear at random player locations, arm after **2 seconds**, and can **imprison** Rageface or Riplimb — use them to stop Rageface from **Face Rage**-ing the raid or to keep Riplimb from fetching the spear. **Immolation Traps** (fire patches) exist on **Normal and Heroic** — they are **not** Heroic-only. **Normal:** both dogs must **die before Shannox reaches ~30% HP** or they **enrage** and one-shot the raid — manage dog HP carefully. **Heroic:** dogs **do not** need to die; **trap** them with **Crystal Prison** to **reset their stacking buffs** instead of juggling execute ranges. Land a **40k+ crit** on **Rageface** while he is channeling **Face Rage** on someone to break the channel. Because you ignore dog HP thresholds, **Heroic is often easier** logistically: focus boss damage and trap discipline. **Jagged Tear** still matters for tanks.

| Phase | Goal |
|--------|------|
| **Add phase** | **[N]** Kill/trap dogs before boss 30%. **[H]** Trap dogs on cooldown to reset stacks; crit Rageface out of Face Rage; mostly DPS boss. |
| **Burn** | Burn Shannox; avoid traps/spear. **[N]** Dogs must die first. **[H]** Dogs can stay alive in traps — fewer Frenzy complications. |

| Unit | **[N]** Normal | **[H]** Heroic |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| **Shannox** | ~12.7M (10) / ~39.1M (25) | ~19.5M (10) / ~59.5M (25) |
| **Riplimb** | ~1.4M (10) / ~4.8M (25) | ~2.1M (10) / ~7.1M (25) |
| **Rageface** | ~1.4M (10) / ~4.8M (25) | ~2.1M (10) / ~7.1M (25) |

- **Hurl Spear** [N/H]: Shannox hurls his spear (Physical + Fire damage in an area). **Riplimb** runs to fetch it and returns it to Shannox. While Riplimb is **trapped** (Crystal Prison), he can't fetch — Shannox remains without his spear and does reduced damage.
- **Crystal Prison Trap** [N/H]: Shannox places traps at **random player locations**. They **arm after 2 seconds**. When armed, they **immobilize** the first unit that steps in (player, Rageface, or Riplimb). Use them to trap **Rageface** (stop Face Rage) or **Riplimb** (stop spear fetch). **[H]** Also use prisons to **reset Fury / stacking buffs** on dogs without killing them.
- **Frenzy** [N/H]: When **Rageface** or **Riplimb** dies, Shannox gains **Frenzy**: +**30% Physical damage** and +**30% attack speed**. Stacks **twice** (up to **60%**) if both dogs die. **[N]** Plan dog deaths before boss 30%. **[H]** You usually **avoid killing dogs** entirely — Frenzy is rarer, but still know what happens if a dog accidentally dies.

## **SHANNOX** [N/H]

- **Hurl Spear:** Throws spear at a location; **Riplimb** fetches it. When Riplimb returns the spear, Shannox gets it back and hits harder. **Trap Riplimb** in Crystal Prisons when the spear is down so he can't fetch.
- **Arcing Slash:** Cleave attack that applies **Jagged Tear** (stacking Physical DoT, ticks every ~3 s, lasts ~24–30 s). Tanks must manage stacks; use defensives when high.
- Tank Shannox **away from the raid** to avoid cleave. Position so Rageface and Riplimb can be kited over traps.

---

## **RIPLIMB** [N/H]

- **Runs to Hurl Spear** and brings it back to Shannox. While he's **trapped** (Crystal Prison), he can't fetch — Shannox stays without spear.
- Can be **tanked** or **kited**. His attacks also apply **Jagged Tear**. DPS when safe; priority is often to **trap him** so Shannox doesn't get the spear, then kill Rageface, then Riplimb or Shannox depending on strategy.
- **[H]** Higher HP and damage.

---

## **RAGEFACE** [N/H]

- **Face Rage:** Rageface **fixates** on random raid members (cannot be tanked in the traditional sense). He **leaps** at them, stunning and dealing **heavy Physical damage** that increases over time. If he stays on the same target, he applies **Rage** stacks.
- **Rage:** At **5 stacks** (or equivalent), Rageface **devours** the target (death). **Tank swap** isn't possible (he's not tanked); use **Crystal Prison Traps** to imprison him and break his fixate, or **kill Rageface** before stacks reach 5.
- **Strategy:** Lure Rageface over an **armed** Crystal Prison so he gets trapped; DPS him while he's locked. Or burn him down quickly so the raid can focus Shannox and Riplimb.
- **[H]** **Face Rage break:** While Rageface is chewing a player, land a **single hit ≥ ~40k** (crit helps) — this **frees** the victim instantly. Combine with traps if the crit fails.
- **[H]** More damage; traps matter more than dog HP micromanagement.

---

## **TRAPS** [N/H]

- **Crystal Prison Trap** [N/H]: Placed at random player locations. **Arms after 2 seconds.** Immobilizes first unit that steps in. Use to trap **Rageface** (stop Face Rage / devour) or **Riplimb** (stop spear fetch). Don't stand in them yourself once armed unless intentional.
- **Immolation Trap** [N/H]: Fire trap on the ground; **avoid**. Deals heavy Fire damage on **both** difficulties. Position so dogs or Shannox don't force the raid through them.

---

## **JAGGED TEAR & TANKING** [N/H]

- **Jagged Tear** is applied by **Shannox's Arcing Slash** and **Riplimb's** attacks. It's a stacking **Physical DoT** (ticks every ~3 s, duration ~24–30 s). Tanks should use **defensive cooldowns** when stacks get high and coordinate healer attention.
- **Kill order** options: (1) **Rageface first** — stops devours, then Riplimb, then Shannox (one Frenzy stack). (2) **All three low, then simultaneous kill** — minimizes Frenzy time but requires coordination. (3) **Riplimb first** — Shannox never gets spear back; then Rageface, then Shannox.

---

## **HEROIC-ONLY MECHANICS** [H]

- Higher boss/add **HP** and damage; **trap management** (Crystal + Immolation) is the skill check, not dog execute ranges.
- **Dog management** is about **trapping to reset stacks** and **40k Face Rage breaks**, not killing dogs before 30%.

---

## **STRATEGY SUMMARY**

**Tanks** [N/H]
- One tank: **Shannox** (avoid Arcing Slash cleave on raid; manage Jagged Tear).
- One tank: **Rageface** if your strategy tanks him when possible, or offtank **Riplimb**; otherwise kite Rageface over traps. **Swap** or **trap** Rageface so no one gets 5 Rage stacks (devour).
- Position so **Riplimb** can be trapped by Crystal Prisons; DPS or kite as needed.

**DPS** [N/H]
- Priority: often **Rageface** (stop devours) or **Riplimb** (stop spear fetch), then **Shannox**. Or bring all three low and kill simultaneously.
- Avoid spear impact; don't stand in **Crystal Prison** or **[H] Immolation Trap** once armed.

**Healers** [N/H]
- Heal tank damage (Jagged Tear stacks); watch for **Rage** stacks and **Face Rage** burst. **[H]** More damage; Frenzy phase is intense.
