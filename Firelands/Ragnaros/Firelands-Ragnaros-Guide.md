# **FIRELANDS — RAGNAROS**
## Boss guide · Normal + Heroic · Discord-ready

**Tag key:** **[N]** = Normal only · **[H]** = Heroic only · **[N/H]** = Both

---

## **OVERVIEW**

Ragnaros is the **final boss** of Firelands. He stands in **lava** in the center; the raid fights from the platform. **Phase 1:** DPS Ragnaros; avoid **Sulfuras Smash** (massive damage in a zone), **Magma Traps** (trigger or avoid — erupt for heavy Fire in 8 yd), **Wrath of Ragnaros** (knockback — don't get knocked into lava), and **meteors**/targeted damage. At **70%** health he **submerges**: **Sons of Flame** spawn and run toward the **hammer (Sulfuras)**. **Kill all Sons before they reach it** or they cause **Supernova** (massive raid damage / wipe). Submerge lasts **45 seconds** or until all Sons are dead. He then **returns** — Phase 2 (same mechanics, burn). Cycle repeats. **[H]** At **10%** health he **despawns** and **reappears at 50%** health (effectively **140%** total health to kill). **[H] Lava Wave:** Waves of lava cross the platform; **move** between waves or one-shot.

| Phase | Goal |
|--------|------|
| **Phase 1 (70%–100%)** | DPS Ragnaros; avoid Smash, traps, knockback; handle adds if any. |
| **Submerge (70%)** | Sons of Flame spawn; kill all before they reach Sulfuras (45 s or until dead). Stuns, slows, burst. |
| **Phase 2 (return)** | Same mechanics; burn. **[H]** Lava Waves — move to safe spots. **[H]** At 10% he leaves and returns at 50% — burn again. |

## **HEALTH & TIMERS**

| Unit | **[N]** Normal | **[H]** Heroic |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| **Ragnaros** | ~43M (10) / ~132M (25) | ~65M (10) / ~199M (25) *(+ 50% respawn at 10%)* |
| **Sons of Flame** | ~500k+ each (8 spawn) | Higher HP/damage; faster or more Sons |

- **Submerge** [N/H]: At **70%** health Ragnaros **submerges**. **8 Sons of Flame** spawn and run toward **Sulfuras** (the hammer). If a Son reaches it, **Supernova** — **~125,000 Fire** to entire raid (wipe). Submerge lasts **45 seconds** or until **all Sons are dead**. Then he returns.
- **Sons of Flame** [N/H]: Move **75% faster** for every **5%** of their health **above 50%** (i.e. slow them by damaging them). Use **stuns, slows, burst DPS**. Kill before they reach center.
- **[H] Respawn:** At **10%** health Ragnaros **despawns** and **reappears at 50%** health. You must deal **140%** of his total HP in damage over the fight.

---

## **PHASE 1 — IN LAVA** [N/H]

- Ragnaros is in the center in lava. Raid DPS from the platform.
- **Sulfuras Smash** [N/H]: He slams Sulfuras at a **random player's location**. **~550,000 Fire** to players within **5 yards** of impact. **Move out** of the marked zone before impact.
- **Magma Trap** [N/H]: He throws **Magma Traps** at random locations. When **stepped on**, they **erupt** for **~80,000 Fire** in an **8-yard** radius. Trigger deliberately at the edge with one player, or avoid.
- **Wrath of Ragnaros** [N/H]: **Knockback** on the raid. Position so you are **not knocked into lava** (stand with back to safe direction).
- **Meteors / Lava Bolt** [N/H]: Targeted or AoE Fire damage; spread or stack as required. Healers keep raid topped.

---

## **SUBMERGE** [N/H]

- Ragnaros **submerges** at **70%**. **8 Sons of Flame** spawn and run toward **Sulfuras**.
- **Kill all Sons** before they reach the hammer. They move faster when above 50% HP — **damage them to slow**, use **stuns and slows**, burst DPS. If any Son reaches Sulfuras, **Supernova** wipes the raid.
- After **45 seconds** or when all Sons are dead, Ragnaros **returns** — Phase 2 begins.

---

## **PHASE 2 (RETURN) & LATER** [N/H]

- Same mechanics: **Sulfuras Smash**, **Magma Trap**, **Wrath of Ragnaros**, meteors. Burn Ragnaros.
- **Bloodlust / Heroism / Time Warp** often used when he **returns** from submerge or at a set health %.
- **[H] Lava Wave:** Waves of lava sweep across the platform. **Move** to safe spots **between** waves; staying in a wave is lethal.
- **[H]** At **10%** he **despawns** and **reappears at 50%**; burn him down again (no second submerge in that segment, but full mechanics).

---

## **HEROIC-ONLY MECHANICS** [H]

- **Lava Wave:** Must move between waves to avoid one-shot. Learn the pattern; call out safe zones.
- **10% transition / “legs” phase:** When Ragnaros hits **~10%** he **leaves the lava hammer pose** and **stands on the platform** — on Heroic this is effectively a **brand-new phase** (extra voice line, new model, new mechanics depending on client): **Living Meteors** that must be kited to **Dreadflame**, **Breadth of Frost** ice patches used to debuff **Molten Elementals**, **Lava Scions**, **Engulfing Darkness** zones, split raid corners, etc. Your **Bloodlust** plan, healer CDs, and tank positioning all reset mentally here — it is **not** “more of Phase 2.” After this segment he may still have a **50% HP re-engage** on some versions — check your server’s script — but always teach the **platform/legs** segment as its own study block.
- **10% respawn math:** Many clients still require **~140% effective HP** because he returns at **50%** after the transition — sustain DPS and healing through both the legs phase **and** the second burn.
- More **Sons** or faster Sons during submerge; stricter DPS check. Higher boss HP and damage; longer fight.

---

## **STRATEGY SUMMARY**

**Tanks** [N/H]
- Position adds (if any); avoid Sulfuras Smash; don't get knocked into lava by Wrath. **[H]** Move with raid for Lava Waves.

**DPS** [N/H]
- Burn Ragnaros. **Submerge:** **Kill Sons of Flame** (stun, slow, burst); damage them to slow their movement. Avoid Smash, Magma Trap, meteors. **[H]** Move for Lava Waves.

**Healers** [N/H]
- Heal through meteors and AoE; watch knockback and lava. Submerge: keep raid up while DPS focus Sons. **[H]** Lava Wave and 10% respawn phase are intense.
