Overview

Ragnaros is the final boss of Firelands. He stands in lava; raid fights from the platform. Phase 1: DPS him; avoid Sulfuras Smash (~550k in 5 yd), Magma Traps (~80k in 8 yd), Wrath of Ragnaros (knockback â€” don't fall in lava), meteors. At 70% he submerges: 8 Sons of Flame run toward Sulfuras. Kill all Sons before they reach it or Supernova wipes the raid. Submerge lasts 45 s or until Sons dead. He then returns (Phase 2, same mechanics). [H] At 10% he despawns and reappears at 50% (140% total HP). [H] Lava Wave: move between waves or one-shot.

Phase 1 — In Lava

  • Ragnaros in center in lava. Raid DPS from platform.
  • Sulfuras Smash: Slams at random player location. ~550,000 Fire in 5 yards. Move out of marked zone.
  • Magma Trap: Erupt when stepped on â€” ~80,000 Fire in 8-yard radius. Trigger at edge or avoid.
  • Wrath of Ragnaros: Knockback; don't get knocked into lava.
  • Meteors / Lava Bolt: Targeted or AoE fire; spread or stack as required.
🔥

Submerge

  • At 70% Ragnaros submerges. 8 Sons of Flame spawn and run toward Sulfuras. If any reaches it, Supernova (~125k Fire to raid, wipe).
  • Kill all Sons before they reach the hammer. They move faster above 50% HP â€” damage to slow; use stuns, slows, burst. Submerge lasts 45 s or until all Sons dead.
  • Then Ragnaros returns â€” Phase 2.
🔥

Phase 2 (Return) & Later

  • Same mechanics: Sulfuras Smash, Magma Trap, Wrath, meteors. Burn Ragnaros. Lust when he returns or at set %.
  • [H] Lava Wave: Waves sweep the platform; move to safe spots between waves or one-shot.
  • [H] At 10% he despawns and reappears at 50%; burn him down again.
🔥

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

  • Lava Wave: Must move between waves to avoid one-shot. Learn pattern; call safe zones.
  • Heroic legs / platform phase: Around 10% Ragnaros stands up with legs â€” treat this as a new encounter: meteors, frost patches, elemental adds, engulfing zones, split corners (exact names vary by client).
  • 10% respawn: He often returns at 50% HP after the spectacle â€” plan for ~140% total damage.
  • More Sons or faster Sons; stricter submerge DPS check. Higher boss HP and damage.

Strategy Summary

Tanks
  • Position adds; avoid Sulfuras Smash; don't get knocked into lava. [H] Move with raid for Lava Waves.
DPS
  • Burn Ragnaros. Submerge: kill Sons (stun, slow, burst); damage to slow movement. Avoid Smash, Magma Trap, meteors. [H] Move for Lava Waves.
Healers
  • Heal through meteors and AoE; watch knockback and lava. Submerge: keep raid up while DPS focus Sons. [H] Lava Wave and 10% respawn phase intense.
