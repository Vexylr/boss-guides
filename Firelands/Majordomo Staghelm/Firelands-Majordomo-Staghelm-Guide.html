Cat Form

  • Leaping Flames: Leaps at a player; summons Spirit of the Flame. Kill the add; spread or assign soaks.
  • Melee on tank; tank swap when debuff (e.g. Feline Fury) stacks get high.
  • Stay spread to keep him in Cat. When he switches to Scorpion, Searing Seeds are placed â€” move seeds away or to safe zone before they explode.
🔥

Scorpion Form

  • Flame Scythe: Frontal cleave; damage split evenly among all players in the cone. Wrong: turning the boss away so only the tank eats it. Right: entire raid (including healers) stands in the cleave with the tank. Aim ~30 stacks (Normal) / ~20 (Heroic) before forcing Cat.
  • Burning Orbs: When he switches Scorpionâ†’Cat, rotate who stands closest to each orb for its 40s chase.
  • Stack (7+) to keep him in Scorpion; spread when you want Cat. One tank is standard.

Human Form (Every Third Transform)

  • Fiery Cyclone: Tosses all players into the air; everyone invulnerable for 3 seconds. Then he returns to Cat or Scorpion based on current raid positioning.
