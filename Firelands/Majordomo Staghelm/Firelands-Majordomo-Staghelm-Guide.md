# **FIRELANDS — MAJORDOMO STAGHELM**
## Boss guide · Normal + Heroic · Discord-ready

**Tag key:** **[N]** = Normal only · **[H]** = Heroic only · **[N/H]** = Both

---

## **OVERVIEW**

Majordomo Staghelm (Fandral) has **two main forms** — **Cat** and **Scorpion** — plus a brief **Human** form on every **third** transformation. Form is controlled by **raid positioning**: when the raid is **spread**, he is in **Cat form**; when **7 or more** players are **clustered**, he switches to **Scorpion form**. Each transformation grants him a stack of **Fury** (+10% Physical damage permanently), so minimize unnecessary form swaps. In **Cat** he uses **Leaping Flames** (summons **Spirit of the Flame**) and **Searing Seeds** when he transitions toward Scorpion. In **Scorpion** he uses **Flame Scythe** — damage is **split among everyone standing in the frontal cone**, so **do NOT turn the boss away**: **tank, healers, and DPS all stack inside the cleave** to soak stacks together (up to **~30** stacks on Normal / **~20** on Heroic before you intentionally force Cat). **Burning Orbs** chase the **nearest** player for **40 seconds** — rotate predetermined soakers; running to Africa does **not** despawn them faster. **Searing Seeds:** only run out with **~3 seconds** left on the debuff (**5s** if nervous), then **snap back** into stack immediately so seeds don’t spread fire everywhere and force an early Cat. **Human** form: **Fiery Cyclone** (3 s air time). Use **one tank** even at minimum ilevel — a second tank is unnecessary padding. Heroic adds the **Concentration** power bar (see below).

| Phase | Goal |
|--------|------|
| **Cat form** | Raid spread. Tank swap on debuff; avoid Leaping Flames impact; handle Spirits of the Flame; move out of Searing Seeds (when he switches to Scorpion). |
| **Scorpion form** | Raid stack (7+). **Everyone soaks Flame Scythe** in the cone (tank faces **into** the raid). Rotate orb soakers; fewer Scythe stacks before swapping to Cat. |
| **Human (every 3rd)** | Fiery Cyclone — brief invuln; then return to Cat or Scorpion based on positioning. |

## **HEALTH & TIMERS**

| Unit | **[N]** Normal | **[H]** Heroic |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| **Majordomo Staghelm** | ~12.7M (10) / ~39.1M (25) | ~19.5M (10) / ~59.5M (25) |
| **Spirit of the Flame** | Add (from Leaping Flames) | Higher HP/damage |

- **Form change** [N/H]: Driven by **positioning**, not health. **Spread** → Cat. **7+ stacked** → Scorpion. **Every third** transform he briefly goes **Human** (Fiery Cyclone).
- **Fury** [N/H]: Each transformation gives Staghelm a stack of **Fury** (+10% Physical damage, permanent). Fewer transforms = less damage.
- **Searing Seeds** [N/H]: When switching **Cat → Scorpion**, he plants **Searing Seeds** on players. Seeds **grow at different rates**; when fully grown they **explode** for Fire damage. **Run with ~3s remaining (5s if safer), then immediately return** to the stack so explosions stay controlled — sitting out too long spreads fire and forces Cat form accidentally.
- **Burning Orbs** [N/H]: When switching **Scorpion → Cat**, he summons **Burning Orbs** that **fixate the closest player for 40s**. Rotate **assigned soakers** into melee range of each orb; do **not** kite them indefinitely — that wastes space and healing.

## **CAT FORM** [N/H]

- **Leaping Flames:** Staghelm **leaps** at a player, dealing damage and summoning a **Spirit of the Flame** add. Kill the add; spread so fewer players take leap damage, or assign soaks.
- **Melee:** Tank takes melee damage; **tank swap** when debuff or stacks get high (e.g. **Feline Fury** or similar).
- **Raid:** Stay **spread** to keep him in Cat. When you want to force **Scorpion**, **stack** (7+). After Searing Seeds are placed (Cat→Scorpion), move seeds away or to safe zone before they explode.

---

## **SCORPION FORM** [N/H]

- **Flame Scythe:** Frontal **cleave** whose damage is **split evenly across every player in the cone**. **Wrong strat:** turning the boss 180° so “only the tank eats it” — the tank will melt. **Correct strat:** **entire raid (including healers)** stands inside the cleave with the tank so each Scythe tick is trivial. Aim for **~30 stacks** on Normal / **~20** on Heroic before you force **Cat** to reset positional pressure (your heal comp adjusts the exact number).
- **Burning Orbs:** When he switches **Scorpion → Cat**, orbs spawn — **rotate soakers** every ~40s; they always chase whoever is closest.
- **Tank:** **Single tank** is enough even in early gear — more tanks just reduce DPS without helping Scythe soak.
- **Raid:** **Stack (7+)** to enter Scorpion; **spread** only when you want Cat. After seeds explode, collapse again quickly.

---

## **HUMAN FORM (EVERY THIRD TRANSFORM)** [N/H]

- Staghelm briefly appears in **Human** form. **Fiery Cyclone:** He tosses **all players into the air**; everyone is **invulnerable for 3 seconds**. Use this to avoid other mechanics if timed well. After that he returns to Cat or Scorpion based on current raid positioning.

---

## **HEROIC-ONLY MECHANICS** [H]

- **Concentration bar (0–100):** Staghelm gains **+25% damage and healing done** for each **25%** of the bar, capping at **+100%** (double) when full. **Any raid damage taken resets the bar.** That is why you send your **two best DPS plus your best healer** to stand **directly behind the boss** during Scorpion — they take the extra **cleave damage** from his higher output but keep the bar climbing. Everyone else soaks Scythe from the front as usual.
- Because his damage scales with Concentration, you will **swap to Cat sooner** (fewer Scythe stacks than Normal) even though the soak group is elite.
- **Flame Scythe / Seeds / Orbs** all hit harder — seed timing and orb rotations are non-negotiable.

---

## **STRATEGY SUMMARY**

**Tanks** [N/H]
- **Cat:** Solo tank; swap only if debuff requires (rare). Position boss for controlled seed drops. Pick up **Spirit of the Flame**; kill add.
- **Scorpion:** **Face the raid** so everyone can stack inside **Flame Scythe**. **[H]** Ensure the **DPS×2 + healer** trio is behind the boss soaking the extra cleave for Concentration.

**DPS** [N/H]
- Kill **Spirit of the Flame** when it spawns. Burn boss. **Spread** when you want Cat; **stack (7+)** when you want Scorpion (if your strategy uses both). Avoid Leaping Flames impact; don't stand in Searing Seeds or Burning Orbs.

**Healers** [N/H]
- Heal tank swaps; watch for Leap damage and Seed/Orb damage. **[H]** Higher damage; cooldowns for Scythe or Leap spikes.
