# **FIRELANDS — BETH'TILAC**
## Boss guide · Normal + Heroic · Discord-ready

**Tag key:** **[N]** = Normal only · **[H]** = Heroic only · **[N/H]** = Both

---

## **OVERVIEW**

Beth'tilac is a **two-phase** encounter. The raid fights **spider adds on the ground** while a **topside team** climbs her web to DPS her. **Cinderweb Spinners** must be killed to drop filaments for climbing. **10-player rule:** at most **four** players may be upstairs — typical split **2 DPS + 1 tank + 1 healer**. A **Blood DK** can solo the web (no healer/DPS up top) if the goal is survival rather than a speedkill. Before **Smoldering Devastation**, everyone must **leave the web**. After three cycles she **descends** — Phase 2 is a **burn race** before **Frenzy** stacks wipe the raid.

| Phase | Goal |
|--------|------|
| **Phase 1** | Ground: kill Spinners (drop filaments), Spiderlings, Drones. Topside: climb web, DPS Beth'tilac, jump down before Smoldering Devastation. |
| **Phase 2** | Beth'tilac on ground. Burn before Frenzy + Consume stacks overwhelm the raid. |

## **PHASE 1 — WEB & GROUND** [N/H]

### **Ground: adds**

- **Cinderweb Spinner** [N/H]: Must be **taunted and killed**. Drops **filaments** — players right-click to **climb the web**. Any **hanging spider** (Spinner/Broodling on the canopy) that is ignored will eventually **cocoon** a random player — **always taunt** them onto the ground team quickly or the cocooned player (often a healer) is removed from the fight.
- **Cinderweb Spiderling** [N/H]: Small adds. **Kill before they reach Beth'tilac** (they empower her or heal her).
- **Cinderweb Drone** [N/H]: High HP. Has **Fire Energy**; if it reaches **0** without being killed, it **explodes** or empowers the boss. **Kill Drones before their energy depletes.**

### **Topside: web**

- **Raid splits (10-player):** **Max four** upstairs — usually **2 DPS + 1 tank + 1 healer**. Larger formats scale up per RL. Rest stay **ground** to handle adds.
- **Beth'tilac** is only damageable **on the web**. Avoid **fire patches** and **web holes** (fall-through).
- **Smoldering Devastation** [N/H]: **Leave the web before she finishes the cast.** Jump down to the ground. Repeat climb each cycle.
- **[H]** More damage, tighter timers; **Widow's Kiss** and add HP/damage increased.

### **Cycles**

- Phase 1 repeats (typically **3** cycles). Each time: ground kills Spinners → topside climbs → DPS boss → **jump down before Smoldering Devastation** → repeat.
- After the final cycle, Beth'tilac **descends** to the ground — **Phase 2**.

---

## **PHASE 2 — BURN** [N/H]

- Beth'tilac is **on the ground** and **tankable**.
- **Tank healing penalty stacks** [N/H]: The boss applies a **stacking debuff** on her tank (up to **10** stacks, **−10% healing received** per stack on reference tuning). **[H]** The debuff also **pulses AoE damage to the raid** while it is active — when stacks climb, the **tank runs out**, the **off-tank taunts**, and the first tank’s stacks fall off (repeat as needed). **Normal** uses the same stacking penalty without the raid-wide pulse — still swap or reset stacks if healing falls behind.
- **Consume** [N/H]: She **consumes remaining adds** to **heal** herself. Clear adds or minimize them before Phase 2.
- **Frenzy** [N/H]: She gains **increasing damage** over time. **Soft enrage** — burn her before stacks are unrecoverable.
- **Bloodlust / Heroism / Time Warp** at Phase 2 start. Healers use cooldowns; tank uses defensives.

## **HEROIC-ONLY MECHANICS** [H]

- **Cinderweb Broodlings** spawn with waves of **Spiderlings**. Broodlings **fixate** players and **detonate** if anyone walks into them — the detonation itself is not the killer; the **AoE slime pool** afterward is. **Rush the broodling**, force the explosion at the edge of the raid, then **immediately scatter** so you only eat a single tick (~40k on reference tuning).
- **Hanging spiders** (Spinner/Broodling on the web) **must be taunted** quickly or they **cocoon** random players until taunted.
- **Phase 2 healing debuff** now **cleaves the raid** — plan **move-out / off-tank taunt** resets (see Phase 2).
- Higher add/boss **HP**, tighter **Smoldering Devastation** windows, and more **Spiderling** waves — clear ground before **Consume**.

---

## **STRATEGY SUMMARY**

**Phase 1** [N/H]
- **Ground tank:** Hold Spinners; position so DPS can kill them for filaments. Tank/kite Drones as needed.
- **Ground DPS:** Kill Spinners first (filaments), then Spiderlings, then Drones. Don’t let Drones reach 0 energy unkilled.
- **Topside:** Climb when filaments available. DPS Beth'tilac; avoid fire and holes. **Jump down before Smoldering Devastation.**
- **Healers:** Split ground/top; topside heals need to drop with group.

**Phase 2** [N/H]
- **Tank:** Face boss away; use CDs as Frenzy stacks rise.
- **Raid:** Lust at start; burn boss. Minimize adds before phase so **Consume** heals less.
