Overview

Beth'tilac is a two-phase encounter. The raid fights spider adds on the ground while a topside team climbs her web to DPS her. 10-player cap: at most four players upstairs (usually 2 DPS + 1 tank + 1 healer). A Blood DK can solo the web without extra heal/DPS if you are not speed-killing. Cinderweb Spinners must be killed to drop filaments for climbing. Before she casts Smoldering Devastation, everyone must leave the web. After three cycles she descends — Phase 2 is a burn race before Frenzy stacks wipe the raid.

⚠

One-shots anyone on the web. Topside must jump down before the cast completes.

Phase 1 — Web & Ground

Ground: adds [N/H]

  • Cinderweb Spinner: Taunt and kill. Drops filaments — players right-click to climb the web. Any hanging spider left alone will cocoon a random player — always taunt them in quickly.
  • Cinderweb Spiderling: Kill before they reach Beth'tilac.
  • Cinderweb Drone: High HP; has Fire Energy. Kill before energy reaches 0 or they explode/empower boss.

Topside: web [N/H]

  • 10-player: max four upstairs (typically 2 DPS + 1 tank + 1 healer). Larger raids scale per RL. Rest stay ground.
  • Beth'tilac is only damageable on the web. Avoid fire patches and web holes.
  • Jump down before Smoldering Devastation completes. Repeat each cycle (typically 3 cycles).
⚠
Coordinate jump: Assign one caller for "drop now" so the entire topside group leaves the web in time.
🔥

Phase 2 — Burn

  • Beth'tilac is on the ground and tankable.
  • Tank healing penalty: [N/H] stacking debuff on the tank (up to 10 stacks, −10% healing received per stack). [H] the debuff also pulses AoE — tank runs out, off-tank taunts, stacks fall off. [N] same stacks without the raid pulse; swap/clear when healing slips.
  • Consume: She consumes remaining adds to heal. Clear adds before Phase 2.
  • Frenzy: Increasing damage over time. Use Bloodlust at Phase 2 start; healers and tank use cooldowns.
🔥

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

  • Cinderweb Broodlings + Spiderlings: Broodlings fixate and detonate — run into them at the edge, let them pop, then scatter so you only eat one slime tick (~40k reference).
  • Hanging spiders must be taunted or they cocoon healers/DPS.
  • Phase 2 healing debuff now cleaves the raid on Heroic — plan move-out / off-tank taunts.
  • Higher HP, tighter Smoldering Devastation, more add waves.
🔥

Strategy Summary

Phase 1 [N/H]

🛡 Ground tank
  • Hold Spinners; position for DPS to kill for filaments
  • Tank/kite Drones as needed
🛡 Ground DPS
  • Kill Spinners first (filaments), then Spiderlings, then Drones
  • Don't let Drones reach 0 energy unkilled
🌊 Topside
  • Climb when filaments available; DPS Beth'tilac
  • Avoid fire and web holes; jump down before Smoldering Devastation
💉 Healers
  • Split ground/topside; topside must drop with group

Phase 2 [N/H]

🛡 Tank
  • Face boss away; use CDs as Frenzy stacks rise
🔥 Raid
  • Lust at start; burn boss; minimize adds before phase so Consume heals less
