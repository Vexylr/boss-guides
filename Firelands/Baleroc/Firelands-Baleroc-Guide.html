Overview

Baleroc is a single-phase encounter that tests healer throughput and crystal soaks. Meta: solo tank â€” the tank’s HP balloons from Blaze of Glory while Decimation Blade always hits for 90% of that tank’s max HP (min 250k). Swapping tanks usually wipes the new tank because blades scale to the wrong HP profile. Shards of Torment force DPS to rotate Torment; healers cycle Vital Spark → Vital Flame on the solo tank. 6-minute hard enrage. [H] Countdown (two players within 4 yd) and spreading Tormented.

Mechanics

Shards & Torment [N/H]

Shards spawn on the closest non-tank. Stack melee in a predictable spot so the crystal appears where you want. Only DPS should take Torment; tanks and healers must never stand in the beam.

  • The shard channels on the nearest player, applying Torment (stacking Shadow damage). Each shard applies ~24â€“25 stacks total.
  • When you leave the shard, you gain Tormented for 40 seconds: +Shadow damage taken, healing done âˆ’50%. Do not take Torment again until it expires; rotate so the next soaker steps in.
  • Soak strategies: e.g. 2 DPS take ~12 stacks each; or 3 DPS take ~8 each; or one DPS with a strong defensive takes 15â€“18 stacks. Higher stacks = more Vital Spark for healers.
  • [H] Tormented spreads to nearby players. Keep Tormented players away from tanks and healers.

Blades & solo tanking [N/H]

  • Blaze of Glory: Applied to the solo tank: +20% max HP, +20% physical damage taken (stacks). Each stack gives Baleroc Incendiary Soul (+20% Fire damage). HP climbs into the millions â€” required for Inferno Blade.
  • Inferno Blade: For 15 s, melee = 100,000 Fire. Solo tank eats swings with stacked HP + CDs.
  • Decimation Blade: For 15 s, melee = 90% of the tank’s max HP (min 250,000) Shadow; cannot be mitigated (can be dodged/parried). Attack speed 100% slower. Be 100% topped between swings. Never pick up the boss mid-fight without Blaze stacks or you die instantly.

Healing: Vital Spark & Vital Flame [N/H]

  • Vital Spark: Direct heals on a player with Torment stacks give the healer Vital Spark (1 per 3 Torment stacks on target). Stack by healing shard soakers.
  • Vital Flame: Heal on a target with Blaze of Glory consumes Spark â†’ Vital Flame: +5% healing per stack consumed, 15 s. When Flame expires, Spark stacks are restored.
  • One healer team builds Spark on soakers; the other uses Vital Flame on the tank. Swap every 15â€“30 s; time with shard phases (~28 s).

Heroic-Only Mechanics [H]

  • Countdown: Baleroc links two players for 8 sec. They must move within 4 yards of each other or both explode for 100,000 Fire to everyone within 100 yd (wipe). Do not run away from your partner.
  • If one linked player has Tormented, moving together can spread it; have backup soakers/healers ready.
  • Tormented spreads to all nearby. Keep Tormented players isolated from tanks and healers (but still able to close for Countdown if linked).
  • Higher boss HP and damage; 6 min enrage is tight. Healer Spark/Flame coordination is mandatory.

Strategy Summary

Tank (solo)
  • Hold Baleroc entire fight; never touch Shards
  • Stack Blaze, rotate CDs for Inferno windows
  • Full-top before every Decimation swing
Shard soakers (DPS)
  • Pre-position so shards spawn in set spot; rotate who stands in beam
  • [H] Stay away from others when Tormented; Countdown: run to linked partner, stand within 4 yd
