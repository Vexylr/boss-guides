Overview

Three bosses on three platforms: Anshal (Life, West), Nezir (Frost, North), Rohash (Wind, East). They share Wind Energy (+1 per second); at 90 energy each casts an ultimate. Travel between platforms via Slipstream (walk into the air lift at platform edges). Damage all three evenly. All three must die within 60 seconds or the first to die casts Gathering Strength and revives to full — wipe. At ~80 energy, most DPS move to Nezir's platform to stack for Sleet Storm (damage split).

☁

Platform Assignments

Rohash (East) — Wind

  • 2 healers + ranged DPS (no melee — hard to melee safely). No auto attack; Slicing Gale on random targets. Tornado — avoid (high damage + knockback). Wind Blast — frontal cone, knockback. At 90 energy: Hurricane — knocks everyone on platform into air, damage + fall; heal through.

Nezir (North) — Frost

  • 1 tank + 2 healers (swap tank/healer with Anshal platform to reset Wind Chill stacks). Permafrost — frontal Frost (face away). Ice Patch under random feet — move out. Wind Chill — +10% Frost damage taken per stack. At 90 energy: Sleet Storm — massive damage to platform, split among all players on platform. Raid stacks here for Sleet Storm.

Anshal (West) — Life

  • 2 tanks, 2 healers, melee + some ranged (AoE). Ravenous Creeper x5 — tank them away, dispel Toxic Spores. Soothing Breeze — green circle heals boss and adds; move boss/adds out. At 90 energy: Zephyr — heals Anshal and adds; kill all Creepers before Zephyr or they heal to full and get buffed. After Zephyr he deals +225% damage for 15s — tank defensives.
☁

90 Energy — Ultimates

  • ~80 energy: All DPS (except minimal stay on Rohash/Anshal if needed) move to Nezir via Slipstreams. Pop raid defensives for Sleet Storm. After 15s channel, return to platforms and continue DPS.
  • Anshal: Kill all Ravenous Creepers before his Zephyr. After Zephyr, tank use defensives (+225% damage 15s).
  • Rohash: Heal through Hurricane and fall damage.
  • Balance boss HP; around 15% use major DPS cooldowns and burn all three. They must die within 60s of each other.
